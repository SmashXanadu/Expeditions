# [The Emerald Dawn & Crew](#the-emerald-dawn-crew)

**The Ship:** Home base. A sturdy exploration vessel built for long hauls and worse seas than the one it's currently in. 

**Facilities:** Basic for now (galley, sickbay, bunks). Grows as the party fills out the Catalogue and unlocks new ones.

## Captain Wren

- **Role:** Captain
- **Behavior:** Quiet, unwavering authority. A single look does more than a shout would. Speaks slowly, low and measured, and thinks several steps ahead of everyone else.
- **Quirk:** Traces constellations on any flat surface when deep in thought. Can't stand clutter.
- **Drive:** Secretly hunting the **Celestial Navigator**, a legendary star chart tied to a vow made to someone they lost. Will bend the ship's course toward it, faction interests be damned.
- **Hook:** Navigation and ancient-maps expeditions, star-aligned ruins, celestial devices, anything that smells like the sky is involved.

## Finn

- **Role:** Quartermaster
- **Behavior:** Boundless energy, rapid-fire talk that swings between boisterous laughter and hushed whispers. Superstitious to a fault.
- **Quirk:** Deeply afraid of calm seas (an omen of ill fortune). Mutters to themselves when handling strange objects.
- **Drive:** Believes a vast, dormant sea spirit sleeps beneath these waters. Every expedition is a chance to gather an offering or clue toward waking or appeasing it.
- **Hook:** Eerie, omen-heavy expeditions: a stone idol that surfaced after a storm, a buoy that rings without wind, a ship graveyard nobody can explain.

## Pip

- **Role:** Medic and Botanist
- **Behavior:** Endless, childlike curiosity. Soft, reedy voice that rises when excited. Deeply empathetic, fusses over anyone hurt or sick.
- **Quirk:** Tastes unknown plants after a quick sniff, safety be damned. Absent-minded to the point of forgetting basic tasks.
- **Drive:** Someone Pip loves, back at the last real port before the Fog, is fading from a wasting sickness nothing in Pip's kit has touched. Convinced the cure is out here, hidden in some plant or creature nobody's catalogued yet.
- **Hook:** Ecological mysteries, a marsh that breathes like it's alive, a bloom that feeds on something it shouldn't, a coral maze that rearranges with the tide.

## Oz

- **Role:** Waystone Magus / Archivist
- **Behavior:** Calm, melodious, formal. Talks to the Waystones like they're listening, because as far as Oz is concerned, they might be.
- **Quirk:** Meticulously catalogs and sketches every Waystone's unique energy signature, convinced no two are ever alike.
- **Drive:** Believes the Waystones are proof of a much larger, ancient ley-line network, and wants to map every inch of it, eventually unlocking travel that doesn't need the ship at all.
- **Hook:** Anything arcane: unstable magic, ley-line distortions, ruins built around ancient devices. If a Waystone's acting strange, Oz already has a theory, and it's probably right.
