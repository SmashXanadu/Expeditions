# [Ashfall Peak](#ashfall-peak)

A volcanic island of black sand beaches and a slumbering volcano, fertile ash-soil jungle on the lower slopes giving way to jagged ridges and obsidian flows higher up.

- **Vibe:** Tropical, fertile, adventurous.
- **Hub:** **Emberside**, stilt-built longhouses over the black sands, running on geothermal heat.
- **Hook:** A honeycomb of obsidian lava tubes runs beneath the whole settlement: a natural dungeon rumored to hold rare gemstones and heat-resistant flora.
- **Occupants:** Vent-Smiths (geothermal smiths), Ashwalkers (jungle guides), Emberside Settlers (explorers, exiles, traders), Iron Legion Garrison (see below).
- **Facilities:** Geothermal Forges, Ashfall Docks, Lava Tube Entrances, Ridge Watchtowers.

## Faction: The Iron Legion

- **Who:** Disciplined mercenaries, elite naval protection for whoever pays up.
- **Leader:** Commander Valerius, treats naval warfare like a game of chess.
- **Ship:** The Crimson Tide.
- **Presence here:** A rotating garrison posted along the ridges above Emberside, watching the horizon under contract. They keep to the heights and rarely mix with the settlement below.
- **Motivation:** Fulfill contracts with total efficiency. The highest bidder controls the waves.
