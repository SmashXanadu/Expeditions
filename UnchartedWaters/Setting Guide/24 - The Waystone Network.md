# [The Waystone Network](#the-waystone-network)

*Moved out of Ch. 2 (The World) into its own page. Appended here for now; belongs earlier in reading order, right after The World, but slotting it in without a full renumber.*

Every expedition runs on **Waystones**, palm-sized crystals that anchor a tether between a location and the player's ship, the Emerald Dawn. The network connecting them is still being built out, one stone at a time, and your players are part of building it. Two things to keep in your pocket as Guide:

- Players can cut a session short on their own terms: everyone touches the Waystone and holds on for thirty seconds, then a flash of light pulls the party home.
- If you let the clock run out instead, the recall isn't gentle. The tether snaps and yanks everyone back mid-scene, ready or not. Use this. A forced recall mid-crisis is a great way to end a session on a cliffhanger that resolves itself by the next one.
