# [Siren's Rest](#sirens-rest)

A tropical paradise of white sands and turquoise lagoons, crowned with ancient pink-marbled ruins that hum with a faint, melodic resonance. The only known Sanctuary Zone in the Uncharted Waters, bound by an enchantment called the **Peace-Bond** that makes violence physically impossible on its shores.

- **Vibe:** Ethereal, relaxed, deceptively civil. A luxury resort built on top of a powder keg.
- **Hub:** **The Gilded Pavilion**, neutral ground where pirate lords, merchant-princes, and desperate explorers share a table. Information is the real currency, and a deal sealed over spiced rum is treated as more binding than any law.
- **Hook:** The Peace-Bond is anchored by the **Siren-Stone**, a monolith at the island's center. Hairline fractures have started appearing in it, and the enchantment is beginning to falter.
- **Occupants:** The Concordant Hosts (etiquette enforcers), Exiled Powers (pirate captains, fallen nobles, and disgraced magnates who can face their enemies here without bloodshed), plus the resident faction below.
- **Facilities:** The Gilded Pavilion, Sanctuary Docks, The Siren-Stone.

## Faction: The Tidal Conclave

- **Who:** An elitist society of scholars and mystics who draw their power from the celestial bodies and the pull of the tides. Reclusive, emerging only when the cosmic balance is threatened.
- **Leader:** High Oracle Selene, a blind seer who navigates purely by the pull of the tides.
- **Ship:** The Silver Crescent.
- **Presence here:** Keepers of the pink-marbled ruins. The only ones who understand how to maintain the Peace-Bond.
- **Motivation:** Study the celestial alignments and harness the moon's power to control the world's tides and currents.
