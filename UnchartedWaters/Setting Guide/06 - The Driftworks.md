# [The Driftworks](#the-driftworks)

A colossal armada of ships lashed together by rope, chain, and makeshift platforms, drifting as one across the open sea. The whole city rearranges itself as vessels come and go.

- **Vibe:** Vibrant, crowded, resourceful.
- **Hub:** The **Grand Flotilla**, a cluster of heavy iron-clad hulls that anchor the city. Home to the Council of Captains, the main foundries, and the Great Winch.
- **Hook:** The city follows a predictable circular path, locked to the world's current, but the internal layout is chaos. A tavern next to your lodgings this morning might drift three blocks away by sunset.
- **Occupants:** Harbormaster Coil (tracks ship movement and berthing), the Council of Captains (rotating shipmaster government), Scrap-Kin Crews (salvagers on the lower decks).
- **Facilities:** The Great Winch, The Lash Markets, Drywake Forges.

## Faction: The Black Fleet

- **Who:** Pirates who don't consider themselves criminals. In their eyes, the real frauds are the merchants and warlords who claim to own open water in the first place.
- **Leader:** Captain Vane the Shadow, known for boarding ships without firing a single cannon.
- **Ship:** The Black Talon.
- **Presence here:** Hides vessels within the armada. The ever-shifting layout is perfect for vanishing or fencing stolen goods.
- **Motivation:** Dismantle the Gilded Union's grip on the ocean. Live free of any law or king.

## Faction: The Horizon Chasers

- **Who:** A fellowship of explorers who believe the ocean has no end, only undiscovered beginnings.
- **Leader:** Chief Cartographer Elara, obsessed with filling the blank spots on the world map.
- **Ship:** The Cobalt Needle.
- **Presence here:** Uses the Driftworks as a mobile headquarters. Since the armada travels, expeditions launch from a new coordinate every month.
- **Motivation:** Document every island, reef, and current in the world to make passage safe for everyone.
