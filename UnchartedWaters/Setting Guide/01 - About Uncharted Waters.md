# [About Uncharted Waters](#about-uncharted-waters)

## What Is This Book?

This is the **Game Master (GM) / Guide-facing setting zine** for *Uncharted Waters*, an open-table exploration campaign set on a world being mapped anew. 

Generations ago, something happened. The details are lost, even to the people living here now, and the ocean sealed itself behind a ring of impassable fog with a churning magical whirlpool at its heart. Ships are only now daring back out to see what's left.

## What System Do I Use?

Any of them. This book is system agnostic: no stat blocks, no target numbers, nothing mechanical on these pages. Every location, faction, and hook is written to drop into whatever fantasy RPG your table already plays.

That said, it was built hand in hand with **Expeditions**, our own fast, low-prep tabletop RPG, and the two are designed to fit together. If you don't already have a system you love, grab the **Expeditions: Player Rules** and **Expeditions: Guide Rules** booklets and run Uncharted Waters with those.

## What You'll Find Inside

- **The World**: the pitch, the map, and the ship your players actually start on.
- **Locations & Factions**: six ports of call and the eight factions who call them home. Each location gets a fast overview spread, then each faction gets a deeper dive into its leader, its ship, and what it wants from your players.
- **Running the Game**: a starter bestiary and a GM toolkit of ready-made hooks, so you can improvise a session without inventing one from scratch at the table.
## How to Use It

Skim the beginning of this zine through the **Emerald Dawn** section once before your first session so the pitch is second nature. After that, this is a reference, not a novel. When your players decide where they're sailing, flip to that location, steal the hook on the page, and go.
