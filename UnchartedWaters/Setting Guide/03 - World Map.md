# [World Map](#world-map)

![Uncharted Waters Map]({{site.baseurl}}/images/Maps/Blank%20Map.png)

The sea is a circle, and every boundary on it matters.

The outer edge is the **Great Fog**, the wall of mist that seals the world in. Nobody has sailed through it and come back to say what's on the other side.

Between the Fog and the center runs a **constant current**, a slow circular pull that drags anything adrift along the same wide arc. It's why the Driftworks can be a whole floating city and still keep a predictable route. 

At the very center is the **Deep One**, the whirlpool no sane captain sails toward on purpose. Nothing that's gone in has come back out.
