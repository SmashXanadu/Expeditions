# [The Catalogue](#the-catalogue)

*Moved out of Ch. 2 (The World) into its own page. Appended here for now; belongs earlier in reading order, right after The Waystone Network, but slotting it in without a full renumber.*

Between expeditions, the crew logs what they found in the **Catalogue**, a shared record of locations, creatures, species, materials, and artifacts. This is your reward lever as Guide: a good writeup or a physical sample can earn bonus experience, gold, new crafting recipes, or even a new facility aboard the Emerald Dawn. Use it to reward players who engage with the world rather than just the encounter in front of them.
