# [Sunbloom Key](#sunbloom-key)

A tiny ring of sun-bleached stone barely rising above the waves, surrounded by hundreds of floating farm platforms tethered to the seabed by rope, chain, and living coral anchors.

- **Vibe:** Gentle, industrious, improbably hopeful.
- **Hub:** **The Mooring Ring**, a dock-town built on the island's single patch of solid ground: storage silos, drying racks, and seed vaults, with rope-bridges and skiffs running out to the drifting fields.
- **Hook:** The farms depend on a delicate balance of tide, current, and buoyant soil. A recent shift in the currents has begun dragging crop platforms out of alignment, threatening to scatter one of the only reliable food sources in the sea. The Gilded Union, the harvest's only legal buyer, is suddenly very interested in a fix, for reasons that may not match the farmers' own.
- **Occupants:** Current-Farmers, Tidewrights (anchor and framework engineers), Seed-Keepers (guard rare plant strains), plus the resident faction below.
- **Facilities:** Floating Field Lattices, Buoyancy Forges, Driftstore Vaults.

## Faction: The Gilded Union

- **Who:** A massive, bureaucratic network of trade lords who believe gold is the only true law. Controls the flow of essential resources across the world.
- **Leader:** High Factor Aurelia, values a silver tongue as much as a sharp sword.
- **Ship:** The Golden Sovereign.
- **Presence here:** Holds exclusive contracts on the harvest, the only buyer permitted to trade with the Current-Farmers and Seed-Keepers. Not ownership, but close enough.
- **Motivation:** Monopolize the trade of rare spices, fresh water, and luxury goods across every known territory.
