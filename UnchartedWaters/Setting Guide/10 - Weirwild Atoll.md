# [Weirwild Atoll](#weirwild-atoll)

A massive, ancient forest growing straight out of a shallow reef, colossal roots knotted together above and below the waterline. There is no solid ground here, only a living lattice of roots, shadowed pools, and glowing growths beneath a dense canopy.

- **Vibe:** Primal, claustrophobic, bioluminescent.
- **Hub:** **High Weir**, a village suspended in the upper branches. Travel is by canoe through submerged root-tunnels at low tide, or by bridge and platform above.
- **Hook:** The tides here are extreme. Low tide reveals lightless caverns, ancient ruins, and shipwrecks tangled in roots for centuries. High tide swallows the lower village whole, forcing all life upward into the canopy.
- **Occupants:** Canopy Wardens (maintain the ropeways and platforms), Driftbound Survivors (claimed by the atoll, unable or unwilling to leave), plus the resident faction below.
- **Facilities:** High Weir Platforms, Root-Tunnel Docks, Glow Groves.

## Faction: The Verdant Circle

- **Who:** A collective of guardians who see the ocean as a single living organism to protect from exploitation. Travel in organic vessels, using spirit-magic to command sea life and currents against polluters.
- **Leader:** Arch-Druid Thalassa, whose skin has begun to take on the texture of polished driftwood.
- **Ship:** The Verdant Wake.
- **Presence here:** Resides within the atoll's living lattice, tending the shadowed pools and keeping the canopy a sanctuary for the world's oldest sea-spirits.
- **Motivation:** Restore the ocean's balance by purging "dead-water" (pollution) and protecting endangered leviathans.
