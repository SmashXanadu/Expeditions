*New to the campaign? Check out the **[Gazetteer]({{site.baseurl}}/UnchartedWaters/Gazetteer/#uncharted-waters)** first for a look at the world — then come back here to get set up.*

---

## What to Expect

- **Schedule:** We play **every week** on **[Day] at [X PM] for [Y hours]**. We only skip if there's an emergency or fewer than two players sign up.
- **Evolving Rules:** The game system is a work in progress and we're always refining it. Your feedback helps shape Expeditions!

## What the Other Players Expect from You

- **Sign Up:** Keep an eye on Discord for weekly game events. Grab an open spot (2–5 players per session) so we know who's coming.
- **Bring a Character:** Use one you've already made, or come with an idea and we'll build one together. Character creation is quick — we can help you get started.
- **Be Present:** Put away distractions. Your focus keeps the story moving and makes the session better for everyone.
- **Be Inclusive:** Welcome new players, listen to everyone's ideas, and keep the table a positive place.

## Discord

All coordination happens on Discord. Reach out to **Will** for an invite.

Once you're in, you'll have access to:
- **#player-chat** — General out-of-character discussion.
- **#the-emerald-dawn** — In-character chat and downtime roleplay between sessions.
