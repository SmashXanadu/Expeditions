### Ashfall Peak

A volcanic island with black sand beaches and a vast, slumbering volcano at its heart. Fertile ash-soil jungles blanket the lower slopes, while higher elevations give way to jagged ridges, obsidian flows, and wind-scoured stone.

- **The Vibe:** Tropical, fertile, and adventurous.
- **The Hub:** **Emberside**, a collection of longhouses built on stilts above the black sands. The locals harness geothermal vents for smithing, cooking, and heating. It feels like a hard-edged frontier outpost, the last reliable haven for ships remapping the waters beyond.
- **The Hook:** Beneath the island lies a honeycomb of obsidian lava tubes formed by ancient flows. These tunnels create a sprawling natural dungeon directly under the settlement, rumored to contain rare gemstones, heat-resistant flora, and things that never fully cooled.

---------
### Occupants

- **Vent-Smiths**: Skilled artisans who work metal using geothermal heat, producing blades and tools prized across the sea.
- **Ashwalkers**: Jungle guides and hunters adapted to the island’s shifting terrain and toxic fumes.
- **Emberside Settlers**: A mix of explorers, exiles, and traders drawn by opportunity and the island’s untapped wealth.
- **Iron Legion Garrison**: A rotating watch posted along the ridges and obsidian flows high above Emberside, paid to keep an eye on the horizon. They rarely descend to the settlement, and the locals return the favor.

-------------
### Facilities

- **Geothermal Forges**: Open-air smithies built around natural vents, their glow visible even at night.
- **Ashfall Docks**: Reinforced piers driven deep into volcanic sand, designed to survive tremors and shifting tides.
- **Lava Tube Entrances**: Marked (and unmarked) access points into the obsidian tunnels, some maintained, others sealed or forgotten.
- **Ridge Watchtowers**: A chain of Iron Legion lookout posts strung along the wind-scoured heights, built for range rather than comfort.
