# [The Iron Legion](#the-iron-legion)
*Mercenaries*

A highly disciplined military corporation, the Iron Legion provide elite protection and naval enforcement for whoever has the coin to pay. They operate out of fortified sea-bastions, maintaining a fleet of heavy warships that prioritize tactical superiority and overwhelming firepower.

---

- **Ship:** [The Crimson Tide]({{site.baseurl}}/UnchartedWaters/Locations/Ships/TheCrimsonTide/#the-crimson-tide)
- **Leader:** Commander Valerius, a scarred veteran who treats naval warfare like a game of chess.
- **Location:** **Ashfall Peak** — The Legion uses the jagged ridges and obsidian flows as a natural fortress. They have established high-elevation watchtowers on the wind-scoured stone to monitor the surrounding horizon for miles.
- **Motivation:** To fulfill contracts with absolute efficiency, ensuring that the highest bidder maintains control over the waves.
- **Hook:** The Crimson Tide is currently under contract to hunt down a legendary sea monster, but they are looking for "disposable" scouts to lure it to the surface.
