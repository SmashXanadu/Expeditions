## [Steam Crab](#steam-crab)

**Type:** Beast | **Size:** Medium

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 3  | 2   | 12  | 12  | 13  | 12  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Claw** | Snapping strike with an oversized claw. |
| **Drown** | TN12 or grabbed by the leg, knocked prone, and held underwater. |
| **Steam** | TN12 or 2 damage and blinded for one round. |

**Description:**
- Squat crimson shell, oversized claws, steam-venting funnels along its back
- Skitters sideways with surprising speed
- Equally at home in shallow water or on wet stone

**Tactics:**
- Opens with Steam to blind, then closes with claws
- Uses Drown on prone or isolated targets
- Immune to missile fire — rushes archers
- Slows if doused with cold liquid; retreats to water when badly hurt

| Consequences | Minor | Major |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **Attacking** | Take 1 damage from counter-snap<br>Drop your weapon<br>Disadvantage on next attack | Claw crushes your weapon hand. Permanent disadvantage on attack rolls. |
| **Defending** | Take 1 damage<br>Lose a piece of gear to a claw snap<br>Disadvantage on next action | Scalded to the bone by steam. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Sneaking** | Shell crunch underfoot. Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next stealth roll | Caught in a Steam blast from behind. Permanent stealth disadvantage near Steam Crabs. |
| **Fleeing** | Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next action | Caught and dragged underwater. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Interacting** | Lose a trade item to a curious claw<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next social roll | Marked by scent gland. Steam Crabs always detect you automatically. |

| Loot (1d20) | Result |
|:--:|:--|
| 1–8 | Crab meat, a handful of coins from the shallows |
| 9–14 | Intact shell plate (armor component), pressurized steam gland |
| 15–18 | Enchanted claw (functions as a +1 melee weapon) |
| 19–20 | Inner heat gem (holds warmth indefinitely) / map scratched into shell leading to a hot spring |
