# Captain Wren Expeditions

**Theme:** navigation, danger, ancient maps

Wren’s missions should feel **serious and strategic**.

They often involve:
- risky navigation
- suspicious landmarks
- legendary artifacts

### Typical Hooks

- A strange **star alignment**
- An **unmapped island**
- A **shipwreck carrying charts**
- An ancient **navigation tower**

### Hidden Agenda

Wren is **searching for the Celestial Navigator**.

So occasionally:
- they push the ship toward **ancient observatories**
- they investigate **celestial devices**
- they follow **strange star patterns**

### Expedition Example Seed

**The Skyfall Reef**  
A meteor fell into the sea and formed a strange crystal reef.

# Finn Expeditions

**Theme:** superstition, omens, strange artifacts

Finn’s expeditions should feel **mysterious and slightly eerie**.

They often involve:
- cursed objects
- strange tides
- prophetic signs

### Typical Hooks

- bottles washing ashore
- strange carvings
- glowing relics
- unusual animal behavior

### Hidden Agenda

Finn is collecting **offerings for the ancient sea spirit**.

So they subtly guide the crew toward:
- relics
- sacred locations
- strange idols

### Expedition Example Seed

**The Drowned Idol**  
A stone idol surfaced in the tide after a storm.
