What Lies Beneath

**Difficulty:** 1 Star | **Duration:** 1 Hour

## The Beast Beneath the Hull


**The Journey:** A short sail beyond the harbor breakwater...

- Fishing boats drift nervously near the docks, their crews watching the party depart.
- Dark shapes of kelp forests sway beneath the water as the boat glides over deeper sea.
- Something **bumps the hull once** — hard enough to rock the vessel.

### The Location: Open Water

- **Outside:** A small sailboat drifting over deep green water. Jagged reefs sit just below the surface in the distance.
- **Inside:** The boat itself becomes the battlefield — narrow decks, rope rigging, and a mast that can be climbed for advantage.
- **Atmosphere:** The wind whistles through the rigging. Waves slap the hull. Something large circles just below the surface.
    

### The Situation: The Reef Serpent

1 **Reef Serpent** (long, eel-like sea monster) circles the vessel.

- **Health:** 10
- **Damage:** 2 / round
- **Ram** the boat from below, knocking characters off balance.
- **Surface** to snap at anyone near the edge of the deck.
- **Dive** when wounded, circling for another attack.

**Possible Discoveries**

• **Serpent Scar** — The creature bears a deep harpoon wound from a previous encounter.  
• **Broken Net** — A fishing net tangled around its tail, suggesting it has been trapped before.  
• **Glowing Barnacles** — Strange faintly glowing barnacles cling to the creature's scales.  
• **Reef Map** — A floating crate containing an old map of nearby reefs.

**The Clock** : 1 Hour  

If the creature is not dealt with quickly, the serpent **capsizes the boat**, throwing everyone into dangerous open water.

**Escalations**

• **Heavy Wave** — A sudden swell washes across the deck.  
• **Serpent Coil** — The monster wraps partially around the hull, shaking the boat.  
• **Hull Crack** — A loud crack signals the hull beginning to split.

**Rewards**: Fresh Seafood Feast • 6 Gold • Harpoon & Rope
