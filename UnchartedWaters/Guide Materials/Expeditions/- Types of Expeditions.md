### 1. **Survey Expeditions**

Map and document an island or landmark.

Focus:
- navigation
- environmental hazards
- discovery

Examples

- Mapping a floating mangrove island
- Charting a volcanic coast
- Surveying a reef full of shipwrecks

These are **very beginner friendly** adventures.

---

### 2. **Specimen Expeditions**

Capture, observe, or retrieve strange creatures.

Focus:
- tracking
- animal behavior
- non-lethal problem solving

Examples

- Catch a luminous crab
- Study migrating sky rays
- Trap a jungle mimic plant

These work very well with **Discovery Clues**.

---

### 3. **Resource Expeditions**

Gather rare materials.

Focus:
- environmental puzzles
- hazards
- extraction challenges

Examples

- Harvest glowcap mushrooms
- Mine skyglass crystals
- Collect leviathan barnacles

These feed your **ship upgrades system**.

---

### 4. **Ruin Expeditions**

Explore ancient structures.

Focus:
- traps
- puzzles
- lore

Examples

- Sunken observatory
- Storm-worn lighthouse
- Crystal temple

These are great **mid-campaign adventures**.

---

### 5. **Incident Expeditions**

Something went wrong.

Focus:

- urgency
- rescue
- damage control

Examples

- A damaged Waystone beacon
- A drifting shipwreck
- A runaway magical experiment

These create **story hooks**.
