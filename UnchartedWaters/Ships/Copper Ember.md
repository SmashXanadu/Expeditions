# [The Copper Ember](#the-copper-ember)

The Copper Ember is a steam-belching, iron-clad vessel used to transport raw ore and molten metals. It is constantly hot to the touch and smells of sulfur and coal. As a mobile forge, it is equipped with massive furnaces and cooling vents that glow orange in the night.

---

### Associated Faction

The Copper Coalition

---

### Occupants

- **Overseer Bram:** A soot-covered foreman who cares more about production quotas than personal safety. He runs the ship with the same brutal efficiency as the mines.
- **Hammer:** The Lead Blacksmith. Hammer manages the ship's onboard forge, capable of repairing damaged hulls or casting new cannonballs at sea.
- **Steam-Eye Joe:** The ship's head engineer. He manages the volatile boilers and the massive paddle-wheels that drive the heavy iron ship through the water.

---

### Facilities

The ship is an industrial nightmare. It features a high-heat forge, a massive ore hold, and a foundry for refining minerals on the go. The "Bunks" are uncomfortably hot, located directly above the boiler rooms to maximize space for cargo.
