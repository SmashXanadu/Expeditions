# [The Azure Abyss](#the-azure-abyss)

The Azure Abyss is a low-profile, sturdy vessel equipped with massive cranes, heavy-duty winches, and primitive diving bells. Its double-reinforced hull is designed to withstand the immense pressure of the deepest trenches, and it carries powerful floodlights that can pierce the absolute darkness of the seabed.

---

### Associated Faction

The Deep Crawlers

---

### Occupants

- **Foreman Grogori:** A man who spends more time in a custom brass diving suit than out of it. His lungs whistle from years of breathing pressurized air, and his authority is as iron-clad as his ship.
- **Zale:** The Lead Salvage Specialist. Zale has a keen eye for "Old World" tech and can identify a valuable relic through an inch of silt and rust.
- **Dottie:** The ship's engineer and crane operator. She is responsible for the complex mechanical systems that lower the divers into the crushing dark.

---

### Facilities

The ship is a maze of pipes, pressure gauges, and steam-valves. It features a specialized decompression chamber, a workshop for repairing salvaged technology, and a "Wet Deck" where divers prepare for their descent into the lightless belly of the sea.
