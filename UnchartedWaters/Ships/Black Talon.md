# [The Black Talon](#the-black-talon)

The Black Talon is a sleek, black-sailed schooner built for extreme speed and stealth. It sits low in the water, making it nearly invisible at night or in heavy fog. Stripped of excess weight, it is a predatory vessel designed to close the distance and board prey before they can fire a single shot.

---

### Associated Faction

The Black Fleet

---

### Occupants

- **Captain Vane the Shadow:** A pirate known for boarding ships without firing a single cannon. He is a master of stealth and psychological warfare, often appearing as if out of the mist itself.
- **Ratty:** The lookout and rigger. Small and nimble, Ratty can climb the masts faster than any man alive and spot a merchant sail on the horizon miles away.
- **Scarlet:** The ship's quartermaster. She maintains order among the lawless crew and ensures that the "spoils of war" are divided according to the pirate code.

---

### Facilities

The Talon is lean and cramped. Facilities are focused on speed and combat, including a hidden "Smuggler's Hold" for stolen goods and a fast-access armory. The crew sleeps in hammocks slung between the cannons to save space.
