# [The Crimson Tide](#the-crimson-tide)

The Crimson Tide is a heavily armored war-galley with a hull painted a deep, intimidating red. It features a reinforced ram designed to splinter enemy timber and multiple decks of heavy ballistae. Built for tactical superiority, the ship moves with a terrifying, rhythmic precision that signals the arrival of a professional—and violent—force.

---

### Associated Faction

The Iron Legion

---

### Occupants

- **Commander Valerius:** A scarred veteran who treats naval warfare like a game of chess. He is cold, calculating, and demands absolute discipline from his crew.
- **Kaelen:** The Master-at-Arms. A specialist in ship-to-ship boarding tactics, responsible for the training of the Legion's marine detachment.
- **Sarge:** The grizzled ship's bosun. He ensures the heavy weapons are always primed.

---

### Facilities

The Crimson Tide is a floating fortress. It features an extensive armory, a tactical war room for planning engagements, and reinforced battle stations. The "Med-Ward" is functional and sterile, designed for the rapid treatment of combat injuries.
