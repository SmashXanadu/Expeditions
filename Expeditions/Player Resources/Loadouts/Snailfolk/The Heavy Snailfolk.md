## [The Heavy](#the-heavy-snailfolk)
*Break problems with force and refuse to fall.*

**Health:** 5

**You carry the gear of a front-line survivor.**  
You wear barnacle-crusted plate reinforced with layered sharkskin.

---

- **Weapon: Barnacled Warhammer.** Iron warhammer built to shatter shells and crush bone.
- **Signature Item: Tidelock Signet.** Barnacle-encrusted ring that hardens your skin like stone.

---

- **Sharkskin Whetstone.** Abrasive sharkskin strip for maintaining an edge on heavy weapons.
- **Numbing Jellyfish Extract.** Bitter paste that dulls the body's sense of pain.
- **Kelp-Wrapped Iron Chain.** Ten feet of iron links wrapped in hardened kelp.
- **Waterproof Bioluminescent Globes.** Deep-sea organisms sealed in an everglowing orb.
