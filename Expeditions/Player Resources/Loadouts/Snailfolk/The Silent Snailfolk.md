## [The Silent](#the-silent-snailfolk)
*Slip past danger and strike where no one expects.*

**Health:** 3

**You carry nothing that catches the current.**  
Fitted sharkskin armor with soft padding and dark kelp wraps.

---

- **Weapon: Twin Spine Daggers.** Balanced blades carved from sea creature spines.
- **Signature Item: Shell-Pick Tools.** Thin shell-fragment picks for opening even delicate locks.

---

- **Anemone Pin.** Sea anemone-shaped clasp that dampens the sound of your movement.
- **Woven Kelp Line.** Dark braided cord with a collapsible coral grappling hook.
- **Sea Salt Dust.** Fine salt crystals used to reveal tripwires or pressure plates.
- **Concentrated Sea Acid.** Sea urchin venom capable of eating through iron lock tumblers.
