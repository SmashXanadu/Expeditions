## [The Wild](#the-wild-snailfolk)
*Endure the harshest depths and hunt what others fear.*

**Health:** 4

**You carry the means to survive the deep.**  
Salt-worn sharkskin, kelp padding, and hardened leather.

---

- **Weapon: Barbed Bone Trident.** Tri-pronged spear carved from deep-sea bone for hunting.
- **Signature Item: Tide Stone.** Always points to the nearest body of water.

---

- **Sharkskin Wrap.** Heavy treated hide that keeps out cold and damp at any depth.
- **Bioluminescent Spore.** Deep-sea organism in a vial — reliable light in any condition.
- **Predator Salve.** Pungent paste that masks your scent and repels deep-sea predators.
- **Barbed Bone Hook.** Collapsible fishing lance tipped with a hand-carved barbed needle.
