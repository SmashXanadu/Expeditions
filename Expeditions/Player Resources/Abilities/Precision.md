## [Precision](#precision)
*Requires Weapon Specialist*

**Target the weakest point** in a foe's defense, sliding your blade between armor plates or striking vital spots with surgical accuracy. **Control the rhythm of the fight** with calculated feints and strikes that ensure no movement or opening is ever wasted.

- +2 to Attack rolls with your specialized weapon.

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Spin your weapon in a tight flourish, testing its balance and reading its edge.
- Examine a target carefully before committing, reading exactly where to place the strike.
- Adjust your grip and angle with deliberate confidence that signals complete mastery.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Find a microscopic chink in heavy plate armor and drive a blow through it.
- Strike a pressure point that causes a target's weapon arm to go completely numb.

**Utility**
- Flick a thrown weapon out of the air with perfect timing.
- Pin a foe's clothing or armor to a wall or tree with a single precise strike.
- Cut a falling rope, chain, or object at the exact moment required.

**Support**
- Surgically sever a restraint binding an ally without grazing them.
- Use a precise feint to draw an attack cleanly away from a companion.

**Social**
- Perform a display of precision so controlled it earns immediate, visible respect or fear.
- Use the implication of exact, surgical capability to make a point without any violence.
