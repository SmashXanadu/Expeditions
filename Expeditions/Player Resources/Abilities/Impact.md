## [Impact](#impact)
*Requires Weapon Specialist*

**Put your full weight behind every swing** to shatter shields, break bones, and send enemies flying backward. **Deliver bone-jarring blows** that leave targets dazed and unable to recover, turning a single hit into a fight-ending moment.

- +1 **Damage** to all attacks with your specialized weapon.

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Test the weight of your weapon with a heavy swing that dents wood or cracks stone.
- Strike a surface with enough controlled force to leave a permanent, visible mark.
- Let the sound of your weapon cutting through air serve as the only warning needed.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Deliver a devastating strike that sends a target flying backward several feet.
- Slam your weapon into the ground to create a shockwave that knocks nearby enemies off-balance.

**Utility**
- Break through a reinforced door, gate, or barricade with a single mighty blow.
- Smash through a shield or weapon, splintering it with brute force.
- Strike an enemy so hard they collapse to the ground, stunned and unable to act.

**Support**
- Use a ground-slam to force multiple enemies back simultaneously, creating space for an ally to move.
- Smash through an obstacle that was blocking a companion's path or escape.

**Social**
- Demolish an object casually — a table, a wall, a pillar — to make a point no one will question.
- Let the permanence and weight of your strikes serve as an unmistakable negotiating position.
