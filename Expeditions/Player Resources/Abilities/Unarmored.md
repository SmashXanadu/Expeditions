## [Unarmored](#unarmored)
*Connects to: Disciplined - Wild - Brawler*

**Master the flow of movement** to evade incoming danger, navigate obstacles with ease, and strike with sudden, fluid precision. **Embrace your physical limits** to move with a speed and freedom that heavy steel could never permit.

- +1 **Evade**

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Move through a crowded room or dense forest without brushing against a single person or branch.
- Adjust your footing silently in response to a shifting or unstable surface.
- Drop into a ready stance that communicates threat without drawing a weapon.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Flow with an incoming strike, redirecting the attacker's momentum and throwing them several feet past you.
- Slide through the legs of a large opponent to strike them from a completely blind spot.

**Utility**
- Sprint across a wall or up a vertical surface for several steps before jumping.
- Twist your body mid-air to dodge a volley of arrows with impossible grace.
- Balance on the tip of a spear or a thin wire during the chaos of a full-scale battle.

**Support**
- Push an ally clear of a hazard and absorb their momentum without breaking your own stride.
- Move a companion out of a danger zone in a single motion without slowing down.

**Social**
- Demonstrate fluid, effortless movement in a way that earns obvious respect or wariness.
- Move so silently through a space that witnesses question whether you were ever present.
