## [Water](#water)
*Connects to: Moon - Blood - Ice*

**Manipulate water** to flow through spaces, shape terrain, preserve life, or freeze obstacles in place. **Channel shifting currents** to bind movement, redirect force, and control momentum.

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Pull a ribbon of water from a nearby source to wet a surface or fill a cup.
- Clean grime or wash a wound with a controlled flow of water.
- Trace shapes or letters through the air using a thin, directed stream.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Whip a stream of water to disarm an opponent or knock them off balance.
- Form a swirling sphere of water around a target's head to silence and disorient them.

**Utility**
- Walk across the surface of a body of still water without sinking.
- Freeze a small patch of ground instantly, causing anyone on it to lose their footing.
- Construct a bridge or ramp of solid ice capable of supporting a group's weight.

**Support**
- Freeze a wound shut to slow dangerous bleeding on an injured ally.
- Cushion a companion's heavy fall with a rising swell of water.

**Social**
- Pull water from a cup across a table to make a point without touching it.
- Use a still pool to observe a reflection around a corner or through a gap.
