## [Shield](#shield)
*Requires Armored*

**Raise your guard** to block incoming projectiles, soak up heavy blows, and create a pocket of safety for your team. **Hold the line** against overwhelming force, turning the enemy's own momentum into a jarring impact that leaves them staggered.

- +1 **Durability** to your equipped shield

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Angle your shield to deflect sparks, falling debris, or a splash of acid away from your face.
- Rap your shield with your weapon to produce a sharp signal that carries across battlefield noise.
- Present your shield face-forward as a clear, unambiguous statement of defensive intent.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Bash forward with your shield, knocking an enemy several feet backward.
- Use your shield rim to deliver a sharp strike to an exposed face or weapon hand.

**Utility**
- Brace your shield against a devastating strike, completely negating the blow.
- Lock your shield into the terrain, creating immovable cover for several seconds.
- Deflect an incoming arrow, bolt, or thrown weapon harmlessly to the ground.

**Support**
- Raise your shield between an ally and an incoming ranged attack at the last moment.
- Create a mobile wall with your shield to cover a companion's retreat across open ground.

**Social**
- Display your shield's heraldry or markings as proof of allegiance, rank, or authority.
- Use your shield to push through a hostile crowd without drawing a weapon.
