## [Decay](#decay)
*Requires Earth or Nature*

**Infect your foes** with virulent blights and toxic fumes that dissolve armor and sap the will to fight. **Spread a creeping blight** that dissolves obstacles and leaves enemies brittle, weak, and susceptible to the slightest touch.

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Cause mold or rot to spread slowly and visibly across nearby organic matter.
- Sense the age and state of decomposition in an object, structure, or area.
- Accelerate the wilting of a flower or the softening of fruit with a touch.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Infect an enemy with a wasting sickness that weakens their body over time.
- Fill the air with toxic spores that choke and slow every enemy in a wide area.

**Utility**
- Rot through a wooden barrier, door, or rope in a matter of seconds.
- Corrode a metal weapon or piece of armor, weakening it structurally at the contact point.
- Cause brittle decay to spread through a stone wall or metal gate until it crumbles.

**Support**
- Rot away a restraint binding an ally without touching them directly.
- Use controlled decay to quickly clear heavy overgrowth blocking a path or passage.

**Social**
- Make the slow, visible spread of decay a pointed demonstration that needs no explanation.
- Use the quiet implication of accelerated rot to deliver a threat entirely without words.
