## [Weapon Specialist](#weapon-specialist)
*Connects to: Impact - Precision*

**Wield your chosen weapon** with a deep understanding of its weight, balance, and lethal potential in a variety of stances. **Focus your martial intent** to turn any tool into a deadly extension of your will, ensuring every blow finds its mark.

- **1-Handed Weapon**: You may dual wield, attacking with each weapon.
- **2-Handed Weapon**: +2 to Attack rolls.

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Twirl or flourish your weapon with such familiarity that you can sheath it without looking.
- Test the weight and balance of an unfamiliar weapon with a practiced, appraising eye.
- Tap your weapon hilt in a recognized signal pattern to communicate without words.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Execute a perfect parry that leaves your opponent's guard wide open for an immediate counter.
- Perform a sweeping strike that hits three different targets with a single fluid motion.

**Utility**
- Use the pommel or haft of your weapon to non-lethally knock out a guard.
- Strike a small flying object — a tossed coin, a thrown key — out of the air with precision.
- Disarm an opponent with a precise strike, sending their weapon spinning several feet away.

**Support**
- Interpose your weapon to block a strike aimed at an ally fighting beside you.
- Use the flat of your blade to redirect a charging enemy away from a vulnerable teammate.

**Social**
- Make a slow, deliberate display of weapon handling that silences a room without a word.
- Offer your weapon hilt-first as a recognized gesture of trust, submission, or good faith.
