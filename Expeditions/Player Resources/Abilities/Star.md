## [Star](#star)
*Requires Air, Fire, or Earth*

**Call down cosmic fragments** and shimmering space debris to crush distant targets or illuminate the entire battlefield with celestial light. **Unleash a torrent of astral energy** that obliterates the landscape with falling meteors or empowers your allies with the raw magnitude of the void.

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Conjure a faint starlike mote that drifts slowly through the air beside you.
- Feel the orientation of distant stars to navigate accurately without landmarks or sky visibility.
- Leave a slow-fading star mark on a surface as a signal or trail marker.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Call down a small meteor that crashes into a specific point on the battlefield.
- Shower an area with radiant stardust that burns and blinds enemies caught within it.

**Utility**
- Illuminate the entire battlefield with brilliant celestial light, eliminating all shadow.
- Create a floating star that reveals invisible or hidden creatures in its proximity.
- Navigate accurately through complete darkness or featureless terrain using star orientation.

**Support**
- Empower an ally with a surge of cosmic force before a critical moment.
- Create a radiant overhead light source that follows the party through darkness indefinitely.

**Social**
- Summon a cascade of falling stars as a display that commands silence and visible awe.
- Use celestial navigation knowledge to establish credibility with sailors, scholars, or explorers.
