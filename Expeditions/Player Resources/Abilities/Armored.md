## [Armored](#armored)
*Connects to: Shield - Protector*

**Adopt a resolute stance** to become a living wall, weathering physical trauma that would break lesser combatants. **Brace against impact** to hold your ground, shield those behind you, and dictate the flow of the frontline.

- +1 **Durability** to all equipped armor

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Adjust your stance to perfectly distribute the weight of your plate, moving with surprising stability.
- Shift your plating to better protect a specific area you know is about to be struck.
- Tap your gauntlet against your chest plate to produce a sharp signal sound that carries through noise.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Slam your armored shoulder into a door or opponent, shattering it with sheer mass.
- Charge forward like a battering ram, smashing through a creature or obstacle and continuing beyond it.

**Utility**
- Lock your joints and armor to withstand a blow from a giant without moving an inch.
- Plant your armored boots into the ground, becoming impossible to push, pull, or knock prone.
- Reflect the force of a melee attack back at the dealer, damaging their weapon on contact.

**Support**
- Position your armored bulk between an ally and an incoming strike, absorbing the blow.
- Use your armor's presence to hold a narrow doorway or chokepoint against multiple enemies.

**Social**
- Stand at full height in your armor to project authority and deter aggression before it starts.
- Let the sound of your approach — rattling plate and heavy footfalls — do the intimidating for you.
