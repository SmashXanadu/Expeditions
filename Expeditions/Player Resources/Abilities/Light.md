## [Light](#light)
*Connects to: Sun - Renewal - Radiance - Spirit*

**Manifest pure luminescence** to banish shadows, provide hope in dark places, or signal over vast distances. **Project a steady glow** that cleanses the air, reveals the unseen, and marks the path for those who follow.

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Create a mote of floating light that hovers above your hand and follows your movement.
- Briefly illuminate a surface to reveal hidden writing, faded markings, or invisible residue.
- Produce a steady glow that illuminates a small area without heat or flame.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Release a sudden flash that leaves everyone looking at you dazzled and temporarily blinded.
- Mark an invisible target with a halo of persistent light that reveals them even through walls.

**Utility**
- Weave light into a solid bridge or shimmering shield that lasts for a short time.
- Cleanse a gallon of water or a small meal of all toxins and impurities.
- Project a beam of light to signal an ally clearly up to a mile away.

**Support**
- Illuminate the path ahead for a group moving through total darkness or heavy fog.
- Provide a calming, warm aura that eases fear and steadies panicked allies nearby.

**Social**
- Create a radiant personal glow that commands attention and projects authority or divinity.
- Use focused light to send coded signals between buildings or across a battlefield.
