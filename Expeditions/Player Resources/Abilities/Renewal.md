## [Renewal](#renewal)
*Requires Light*

**Breathe life back into the weary** to close bleeding wounds, soothe aching limbs, and restore the will to fight. **Circulate a refreshing energy** that purges poisons and mends the spirit, ensuring your team is always ready for the next encounter.

**Charges:** 3

---

### Basic Actions
*Basic actions do not consume a charge.*

- Close small cuts or ease minor aches with a brief, soothing glow.
- Sense the physical condition of a nearby creature — injuries, fatigue, illness, poison.
- Provide a warming, comforting light that eases distress without requiring a full charge.

### Charged Actions
*Charged actions cost a **charge** to perform.*

**Attack**
- Channel renewal energy so intensely it overwhelms corrupted or undead creatures.
- Use the backlash of denied healing energy to stagger a foe attempting to recover.

**Utility**
- Purge poison or disease from a creature entirely in a single sustained effort.
- Restore vitality to a creature suffering from exhaustion, hunger, or prolonged deprivation.
- Instantly stabilize a dying creature, preventing their condition from worsening any further.

**Support**
- Heal a serious wound on yourself or a nearby ally.
- Restore a fallen ally to their feet with enough recovered strength to continue fighting.

**Social**
- Demonstrate healing ability openly to establish trust and goodwill with a suspicious group.
- Use the offer of healing as a meaningful, concrete gesture in a tense or hostile negotiation.
