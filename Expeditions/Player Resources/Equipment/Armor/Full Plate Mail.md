**Name**: Full Plate Mail
**Type**: Heavy
**Slot**: Chest
**Maximum Durability**: 4
**Speed Modifier**: -4
**Description**: A masterful suit of articulated metal plates, covering the wearer from neck to hip. The ultimate in physical protection, but severely impacts movement.
