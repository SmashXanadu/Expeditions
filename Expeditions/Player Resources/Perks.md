# [Perk Sheet](#perk-sheet)
Spend **Experience** earned during **Downtime** to unlock Perks. All Perks are passive and permanent. Some can be taken multiple times — costs may escalate with each purchase.

---

## [Training](#training)

- ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ **Unlock a new Ability** — Unlock a new Ability Node on the Hero Grid. You may only unlock nodes connected to the starting space or a node you already have.
- ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ **Skill Improvement** — +1 to any skill (includes Attack, Magic, Evade, and Resist) up to a maximum of +5.
- ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ **Skill Training** — Gain a new skill and set it at +1.
- ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ **Endurance Training** — +1 to your maximum Health.
- ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ **Magical Training** — +1 to the maximum Charges of a chosen ability node.

## [General](#general)

- ☐ **In the Zone** — Recover 1 charge from any ability when you roll a natural 20.
- ☐ **Jack of All Trades** — When you roll for a check without applying a specific skill, treat it as +1 instead of +0.
- ☐ **Mentor** — Once per expedition, another player may use one of your skill bonuses for their roll.
- ☐ **Packrat** — Gain 5 additional Inventory slots.
- ☐ **Industrious** — Permanently gain one extra Optional Downtime Activity each Downtime phase. This extra activity can be one you performed already.
- ☐ **Appraiser** — You always know the approximate value and magical properties of any item.
- ☐ **Silver Tongue** — Once per expedition, convince any NPC of one believable statement without a roll.

---

## [Physical Offence](#physical-combat)

- ☐ **Heavy Handed** — +1 damage on attack actions.
- ☐ **Press the Attack** — When you use your Action to perform an attack with your weapon, attack twice.
- ☐ **Deathblow** — A natural 20 on an attack deals +2 bonus damage.
- ☐ **Opportunist** — Deal +1 damage against any enemy that has already been hit this round.
- ☐ **Relentless** — When you reduce an enemy to 0 Health, immediately take an additional attack action (Once per turn).
- ☐ **Cleave** — When you deal damage with a melee attack, the nearest enemy to your target takes 1 damage.
- ☐ **First Strike** — Your first attack each combat deals +1 damage.

---

## [Magical Offence](#magical-combat)

- ☐ **Overcharge** — +1 damage on magic attacks.
- ☐ **Bloodied** — Gain +1 to attack and magic rolls while at half Health or below.
- ☐ **Drain** — When you deal damage with a magic action, recover 1 Health.
- ☐ **Backlash** — When an enemy fails a magic attack against you, they take 1 damage.
- ☐ **Volatile** — A natural 20 on a magic roll deals +2 bonus damage.
- ☐ **Echoing Strike** — When you deal damage with a magic action, the nearest enemy to your target takes 1 damage.
- ☐ **Overload** — When all charges in a node are spent, deal 1 damage to all enemies in your zone.

---

## [Defense](#defense)

- ☐ **Riposte** — When you successfully Evade an attack, deal 1 damage to the attacker.
- ☐ **Last Stand** — Once per expedition, survive a killing blow and stay at 1 Health instead of falling unconscious.
- ☐ **Unbreakable** — Once per expedition, automatically turn a failure into a success on an Evade or Resist roll.
- ☐ **Veteran** — Once per expedition, ignore a complication result.
- ☐ **Thick Skinned** — Reduce all incoming damage by 1 (minimum 1).
- ☐ **Lucky** — Once per expedition, reroll any die and keep the new result.
- ☐ **Stubborn** — When you fall unconscious, you may take one final action before going down.
