## [Shell Warden](#shell-warden)

***You are the armor that walks between your kin and harm.** The Kingdom of Gastropa bred you for endurance. Whether holding a tide gate, standing watch over a noble's chamber, or planting yourself between an elder and danger, you are the last thing an enemy reaches.*

---

**Core Skills**
- **+1 Attack:** Your bonus to physical attack rolls.
- **+1 Magic:** Your bonus to magical spell and attack rolls.
- **+1 Evade:** You avoid incoming danger through speed and sharp reflexes.
- **+3 Resist:** You endure magical forces and effects through will and resilience.

**General Skills**
- **+4 Strong:** You can bend, lift, and crush large objects with ease.
- **+3 Tough:** You can tolerate pain and discomfort that others can't.
- **+2 Leader:** You naturally inspire confidence and trust from others.
- **+2 Intimidating:** You make people fear what you are capable of.
- **+1 Wise:** You know from experience how to handle most situations.
- **+1 Kind:** Your care and generosity toward others knows no bounds.

## [Snailfolk](#snailfolk)

*Few Snailfolk leave the Kingdom of Gastropa, but those who do carry an ancient resilience.*

- **Aquatic:** Breathe and thrive underwater.  
Adjust buoyancy on demand. Out of water **45+ min** → hibernation → **15 more min** → death.
- **Snail Sense:** **Disadvantage** on vision rolls. **Advantage** on smell, taste, and touch rolls.
- **Natural Armor:** Use your **Action** to withdraw into your shell, gaining **Advantage** on Evade and Resist rolls until your next turn.
- **Tentacles:** Reach and manipulate small nearby objects with your tentacles.
- **Resistance to Cold:** Resistant to brief cold. Extended cold → hibernation → death.
- **Weakness to Acid:** Particularly susceptible to Acid damage.
- **Slime Trail:** Leaves a visible, trackable trail.  
Once per Expedition, coat a surface — creatures crossing must **Evade 12** or fall **Prone**.
- **Shell Storage:** Store up to **3 small items** in your shell. Cannot be taken.
