## [The Silent](#the-silent-snailfolk)
*Slip past danger and strike where no one expects.*

**You carry nothing that catches the current.** This kit is designed for slipping through tight spaces, bypassing locks, and disappearing before anyone notices you were there. You wear fitted sharkskin armor with soft padding and dark kelp wraps — light protection that favors silence and mobility over defense.
