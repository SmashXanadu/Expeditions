## [Steam Crab](#steam-crab)

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **Name** | Steam Crab |
| **Type** | Beast |
| **Size** | Medium |
| **Description** | A squat, crimson-shelled crab with oversized claws and armored funnels along its back that hiss and rattle with pressurized steam. It skitters sideways with surprising speed, equally at home in shallow water or on wet stone. |
| **Tactics** | Opens with Steam to blind the nearest target, then closes with claws. Uses Drown on prone or isolated targets. Immune to missile fire — rushes archers. Slows if doused with cold liquid; retreats to water when badly hurt. |

**Abilities:**

| # | Name | Effect |
|:--:|:--|:--|
| 1 | Drown | DC 12 DEX or grabbed by the leg, knocked prone, and held underwater. |
| 2 | Steam | DC 12 CON or 2 damage and blinded for one round. |

| :------ | :-: |
| **HP**  | 3   |

| **Atk** | 12  |
| **Mag** | 12  |
| **Def** | 13  |
| **Res** | 12  |

**Consequences:**

| Context | Minor | Major |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **Attacking** | Take 1 damage from a counter-snap, drop your weapon, disadvantage on next attack | Claw crushes your weapon hand — permanent disadvantage on attack rolls |
| **Defending** | Take 1 damage, lose a piece of gear to a claw snap, disadvantage on next action | Scalded to the bone by steam — permanently lose 1 HP from max |
| **Sneaking** | Drop an item (shell crunch underfoot), take 1 damage, disadvantage on next stealth roll | Caught in a Steam blast from behind — permanent disadvantage on stealth near Steam Crabs |
| **Fleeing** | Drop an item, take 1 damage, disadvantage on next action | Caught and dragged underwater — permanently lose 1 HP from max |
| **Interacting** | Lose a trade item to a curious claw, take 1 damage, disadvantage on next social roll | Marked by the crab's scent gland — Steam Crabs always detect you automatically |

**Loot (1d20):**

| d20 | Result |
|:--:|:--|
| 1–8 | Crab meat, a handful of coins from the shallows |
| 9–14 | Intact shell plate (armor component), pressurized steam gland |
| 15–18 | Enchanted claw (functions as a +1 melee weapon) |
| 19–20 | Inner heat gem (holds warmth indefinitely) / map scratched into shell leading to a hot spring |
