## [Harpy](#harpy)

**Type:** Beast | **Size:** Medium

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 3  | 1   | 10  | 10  | 12  | 11  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Talon Strike** | A diving attack from altitude. If the Harpy attacks from the air this turn, it gains +2 Atk. |
| **Shriek** | TN10 or deafened and confused for one round — the target's next action is determined randomly (d4: 1 attack an ally, 2 move away, 3 drop an item, 4 freeze in place). |
| **Lure Song** | TN10 or compelled to move toward the Harpy on the target's next turn. Only usable when the Harpy is not in melee range. |

**Description:**
- A woman's torso joined to eagle's wings and taloned legs, wild salt-matted hair, a screaming expression carved permanently into its face
- Banks in lazy circles overhead, always watching with a predator's patience
- Found on coastal cliffs, sea ruins, mountain passes, and perched atop the masts of wrecked ships

**Tactics:**
- Opens at range with Lure Song to drag party members away from each other
- Dives on isolated targets with Talon Strike; breaks off before they can retaliate
- Uses Shriek when surrounded or cornered — the confusion creates an escape window
- Nests in high, inaccessible places; fights more desperately if the nest is threatened

| **Interacting** | Lose a shiny or food item<br>Take 1 damage from a warning talon<br>Disadvantage on next social roll | Marked as a potential meal. Permanently hostile with this Harpy. |
