## [Zombie Brute](#zombie-brute)

**Type:** Undead | **Size:** Large

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 5  | 2   | 13  | 8   | 15  | 9   |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Slam** | A massive, pulverizing blow with a fist or improvised weapon. |
| **Grab** | TN13 or grabbed — the target takes 1 damage at the start of each turn until they break free (TN13). |
| **Undying** | Once per combat, when this creature would reach 0 HP, it instead drops to 1 HP and keeps fighting. |

**Description:**
- A human corpse swollen to twice its natural size by dark magic, skin split at the seams, eyes milky white
- Moves with unstoppable, grinding momentum — no dodging, no hesitation, just forward
- Found wherever a necromancer needs something large broken, or wherever death magic has gone wrong

**Tactics:**
- Walks straight into the middle of the group, ignoring attacks on its way in
- Targets the smallest or most isolated party member with Grab, then ignores everything else
- Undying means players must commit — half-measures leave it standing
- Slow but relentless; outrunning it buys time, not safety

| **Sneaking** | It smells the living. Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next stealth roll | Once it has your scent, it always finds you. Permanent stealth disadvantage near Zombie Brutes. |
| **Fleeing** | Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next action | Caught in that grip. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Interacting** | It doesn't interact — take 1 damage automatically<br>Drop an item<br>Disadvantage on next action | Marked by necromantic attention. Permanently hostile with the necromancer who controls it. |
