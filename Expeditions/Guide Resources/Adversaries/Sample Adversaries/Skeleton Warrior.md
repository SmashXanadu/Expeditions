## [Skeleton Warrior](#skeleton-warrior)

**Type:** Undead | **Size:** Medium

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 3  | 1   | 10  | 10  | 14  | 10  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Bone Blade** | A strike from an ancient corroded weapon. |
| **Rattle** | TN10 or frightened — the target must move away from the Skeleton on their next turn. |
| **Reform** | The first time this Skeleton reaches 0 HP, it collapses and reassembles with 1 HP at the start of its next turn. Destroyed permanently by fire, holy damage, or a called shot to the skull. |

**Description:**
- Ancient plate fused to yellowed bone, green fire burning in the eye sockets, joints trailing black tendrils of cold energy
- Moves in jerky, mechanical silence — no breath, no flinching, no hesitation
- Found in crypts, ancient battlefields, and anywhere a necromancer has worked

**Tactics:**
- Advances without fear or hesitation; ignores minor wounds entirely
- Uses Rattle to isolate a single target before the rest close in
- Reform makes it punishing to disengage before the job is finished
- Cannot be intimidated, reasoned with, or bribed; fights until destroyed
