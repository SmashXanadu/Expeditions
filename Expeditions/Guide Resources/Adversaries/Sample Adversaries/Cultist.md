## [Cultist](#cultist)

**Type:** Humanoid | **Size:** Medium

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 2  | 1   | 12  | 12  | 13  | 13  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Dark Blade** | A ritual dagger empowered by dark energy — deals both physical and magical damage. |
| **Hex** | TN12 or cursed for the remainder of combat — disadvantage on one type of roll (Guide's choice). |
| **Sacrifice** | The Cultist deals 1 damage to themselves to immediately reuse Hex or Dark Blade without waiting for their turn. |

**Description:**
- Hooded robes, ritual scarring on hands and face, eyes that reflect light at the wrong angle
- Moves with deliberate, unhurried purpose — no wasted motion, no visible fear
- Found in abandoned temples, underground vaults, and anywhere dark magic has been formally invoked

**Tactics:**
- Opens with Hex on the most capable party member before combat is fully joined
- Uses Sacrifice freely — they do not fear death, they welcome a meaningful one
- Will not surrender; may attempt to complete a ritual even while under attack
- Cultists fight in coordinated silence; they rarely speak during combat

| Consequences | Minor | Major |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **Attacking** | Dark energy lashes back — take 1 damage<br>Drop your weapon<br>Disadvantage on next attack | Cursed weapon hand. Permanent disadvantage on attack rolls near dark magic. |
| **Defending** | A hex catches you — take 1 damage<br>Lose a piece of gear to a ritual seizure<br>Disadvantage on next action | Soul-touched. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Sneaking** | A ritual ward triggers. Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next stealth roll | Marked by the cult's patron. Permanent stealth disadvantage in cult-claimed spaces. |
| **Fleeing** | Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage from a parting curse<br>Disadvantage on next action | Hexed in flight. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Interacting** | Lose something of personal value (not money — something meaningful)<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next social roll | Bound by oath. Permanently hostile with this cult. |

| Loot (1d20) | Result |
|:--:|:--|
| 1–8 | Ritual components (ash, bone dust, dark ink), robes, a ceremonial blade |
| 9–14 | A cult cipher or prayer book, a vial of corrupted holy water (inverts normal effects) |
| 15–18 | A named idol with a minor enchantment, a letter from a cult superior |
| 19–20 | Evidence of a cult network and their next target / a ritual item of genuine power |
