## [Dire Wolf](#dire-wolf)

**Type:** Beast | **Size:** Large

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 3  | 2   | 16  | 8   | 15  | 10  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Savage Bite** | A lunging bite that tears deep. The wolf's high Atk reflects its relentless accuracy. |
| **Knockdown** | TN15 or knocked prone. The wolf immediately bites a prone target as a free action. |
| **Pack Tactics** | If any ally is adjacent to the same target, the Dire Wolf gains +2 Atk. |

**Description:**
- Massive black or grey-furred wolf, shoulder height above a man's waist, amber eyes that catch light like mirrors
- Moves in a low, patient predator circle — never charges until the moment is right
- Found in deep wilderness, mountain passes, and as war-mounts for those capable of taming them

**Tactics:**
- Uses Pack Tactics as the cornerstone of every attack — always positions alongside an ally before striking
- Knocks the most armored target prone first, then bites freely while they're down
- The alpha howls at half HP to call 1d3 additional wolves
- Flees only if every packmate is down — alone, it retreats into the dark and waits

| Consequences | Minor | Major |
|:--|:--|:--|

| **Fleeing** | Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage — it's faster than you<br>Disadvantage on next action | Run down. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Interacting** | Lose a piece of food<br>Take 1 damage from a warning bite<br>Disadvantage on next social roll | Marked as prey. This pack hunts you specifically. |
