## [Goblin Shaman](#goblin-shaman)

**Type:** Humanoid | **Size:** Small

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 2  | 2   | 12  | 10  | 10  | 13  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Hex Bolt** | A crackling dark energy shot. Ranged attack. |
| **Curse** | TN10 or disadvantage on all rolls until the Shaman is defeated or spends a full round lifting it. |
| **Ward** | The Shaman places a protective hex on one ally. That ally gains +2 Def until the Shaman's next turn. |

**Description:**
- Smaller than most goblins, draped in bone fetishes, crude charms, and strips of painted hide
- Sways and mutters constantly, gesturing with a gnarled staff topped with a skull
- Found deep in goblin warrens, caves, or anywhere foul magic pools

**Tactics:**
- Opens with Curse on the most dangerous-looking target before closing to melee range
- Stays well behind other goblins, using Ward to protect the biggest fighter
- Avoids melee entirely — uses Hex Bolt from maximum range
- Flees immediately if left without allies to hide behind

| Consequences | Minor | Major |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **Attacking** | Curse lashes back — take 1 damage<br>Drop your weapon<br>Disadvantage on next attack | Permanently hexed. Permanent disadvantage on attack rolls near Goblin magic. |
| **Defending** | A misfired hex hits you — take 1 damage<br>Lose a piece of gear to a claw swipe<br>Disadvantage on next action | Hexed to the bone. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Sneaking** | Charms jingle underfoot. Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next stealth roll | Detected by spirit-sense. Permanent stealth disadvantage near Shamans. |
| **Fleeing** | Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage from a parting hex<br>Disadvantage on next action | Hexed in retreat. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Interacting** | Lose a trade item to a "blessing fee"<br>Take 1 damage from a testing poke<br>Disadvantage on next social roll | Bound by a minor oath. Permanently hostile reputation with Goblin Shamans. |

| Loot (1d20) | Result |
|:--:|:--|
| 1–8 | Bone fetishes, dried herbs, pouches of ash and pigment |
| 9–14 | A vial of hex-water (splashing an enemy gives them disadvantage on their next roll), a crude spell scroll |
| 15–18 | A cursed idol with a minor enchantment |
| 19–20 | A bound spirit sealed in a cracked gem / a ritual map showing something the goblins consider sacred |
