## [Goblin Scout](#goblin-scout)

**Type:** Humanoid | **Size:** Small

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 1  | 1   | 11  | 7   | 9   | 10  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Stab** | A quick jab with a rusty blade. |
| **Ambush** | TN11 or take 2 damage. Only usable on the first round if the Scout was hidden. |
| **Scatter** | The Scout breaks away and flees. TN9 to pursue or it escapes, often leading pursuers toward larger goblins. |

**Description:**
- Wiry frame, yellow-green skin, wide darting eyes, dressed in scraps and stolen mismatched pieces
- Hunches low and circles the edge of combat, never fully committing
- Found in dark forests, abandoned structures, and the outskirts of goblin warrens

**Tactics:**
- Attacks from cover or ambush if possible; opens with Ambush on the first round
- Falls back and calls for reinforcements if left alone
- Scatters when clearly losing — will lead pursuers toward bigger threats
- Prioritizes unarmored or distracted targets; avoids fair fights entirely

| Consequences | Minor | Major |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **Attacking** | Take 1 damage from a desperate bite<br>Drop your weapon<br>Disadvantage on next attack | Rusty blade nicks a tendon. Permanent disadvantage on attack rolls. |
| **Defending** | Take 1 damage<br>Lose a small piece of gear to a snatching hand<br>Disadvantage on next action | Overwhelmed and stomped. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Sneaking** | Stepped on something the Scout was eating. Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next stealth roll | Outed to the entire warren. Permanent stealth disadvantage near Goblins. |
| **Fleeing** | Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next action | Led straight into a larger goblin camp. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Interacting** | Lose a shiny trade item<br>Take 1 damage from a quick stab<br>Disadvantage on next social roll | Robbed and marked as easy prey. Permanently hostile reputation with Goblins. |

| Loot (1d20) | Result |
|:--:|:--|
| 1–8 | Handful of copper coins, a stolen trinket, scraps of rough leather |
| 9–14 | A crude but serviceable blade, a rough map scratched on bark |
| 15–18 | A stolen ring or brooch of moderate value |
| 19–20 | A carried message revealing a larger goblin operation / a small, perfectly formed gemstone |
