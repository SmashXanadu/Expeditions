## [Orc Warrior](#orc-warrior)

**Type:** Humanoid | **Size:** Medium

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 3  | 2   | 13  | 10  | 13  | 12  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Hack** | A brutal overhead strike with a heavy blade or axe. |
| **Brutal Strike** | TN13 or knocked prone and take 1 additional damage. |
| **Battle Cry** | TN10 or all enemies within range are shaken — disadvantage on their next attack roll. |

**Description:**
- Broad-shouldered, scarred hide, heavy weapons carried with casual ease
- Plants feet and meets enemies head-on; no flanking, no retreating
- Found in warbands, occupied territory, raiding parties, and anywhere a fight is promised

**Tactics:**
- Opens with Battle Cry before closing, spreading disadvantage across the party
- Focuses Brutal Strike on the heaviest armored target — knocking them down changes the math
- Does not retreat; becomes more dangerous and erratic at low HP
- Orcs in a group fight shoulder-to-shoulder — a wall, not a skirmish line

| Consequences | Minor | Major |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **Attacking** | Heavy counter-blow — take 1 damage<br>Drop your weapon<br>Disadvantage on next attack | Arm half-broken by a crushing parry. Permanent disadvantage on attack rolls. |
| **Defending** | Take 1 damage<br>Armor piece dented and torn free<br>Disadvantage on next action | Knocked to the ground and stomped. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Sneaking** | Orcs have keen smell. Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next stealth roll | Scent-marked. Permanent stealth disadvantage near Orcs. |
| **Fleeing** | Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next action | Run down and made an example. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Interacting** | Lose a show of value (weapon or coin)<br>Take 1 damage from a test blow<br>Disadvantage on next social roll | Seen as weak. Permanently hostile reputation with this warband. |

| Loot (1d20) | Result |
|:--:|:--|
| 1–8 | Iron warband coin, rough iron weapon, strips of hide armor |
| 9–14 | A warband token granting passage through their territory, a well-made war axe |
| 15–18 | A trophy weapon taken from a notable enemy, clan markings that open doors with certain groups |
| 19–20 | A battle standard with strategic markings / a sealed orders pouch revealing a warband objective |
