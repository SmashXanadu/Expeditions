## [Troll](#troll)

**Type:** Beast | **Size:** Large

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 8  | 3   | 14  | 10  | 15  | 18  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Club** | A massive swing with a tree limb, boulder, or whatever the Troll is holding. |
| **Regenerate** | At the start of each of its turns, the Troll recovers 1 HP — unless it took fire or acid damage since its last turn. |
| **Swipe** | TN14 or all targets adjacent to the Troll take 1 damage and are pushed back one zone. |

**Description:**
- Mottled grey-green skin hanging loose over slabs of muscle, lank oily hair, arms too long, knuckles dragging
- Rolls its shoulders and knuckle-drags forward with the patience of something that knows it will win
- Found in swamps, highland caves, under bridges, and anywhere with enough prey to sustain it

**Tactics:**
- Wades directly into the middle of the group; uses Swipe to reset spacing in its favor
- Regenerate means players must commit — chip damage doesn't work, only sustained pressure does
- Will focus whoever uses fire or acid — the only things that stop it healing
- Doesn't flee; too stubborn for tactics, too confident in its own durability
