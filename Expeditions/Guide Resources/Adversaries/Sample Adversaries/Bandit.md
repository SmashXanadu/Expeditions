## [Bandit](#bandit)

**Type:** Humanoid | **Size:** Medium

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 3  | 1   | 10  | 10  | 14  | 11  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Slash** | A practiced sword or axe strike. |
| **Dirty Fighting** | TN10 or blinded, disarmed, or tripped — Guide's choice based on the situation. |
| **Hold Up** | Social attack. TN10 or the target freezes and loses their action this round. |

**Description:**
- Mismatched armor, road-worn clothes, a mix of hard experience and desperation in equal measure
- Weight shifts forward, blade held low — someone who has done this before
- Found on roads, in ruined inns, at forest crossings, and inside smuggler dens

**Tactics:**
- Prefers to intimidate first with Hold Up before committing to a fight
- Fights defensively in groups; spreads out to avoid being surrounded
- Surrenders or flees at half HP if the odds have clearly turned
- May have information, debts, or a reason to negotiate — not always an enemy
