## [Giant Spider](#giant-spider)

**Type:** Beast | **Size:** Medium

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 3  | 2   | 10  | 8   | 10  | 8   |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Bite** | Fangs sink deep — on a hit, TN10 or take 1 additional damage from venom. |
| **Web** | TN10 or restrained until the target breaks free (TN10 on their turn). |
| **Ambush** | If the Spider attacks from above, from hiding, or through a web-obscured area, it gains +3 Atk on that attack. |

**Description:**
- Black or dark brown body the size of a large dog, eight glinting eyes arranged in two rows, fangs dripping clear fluid
- Drops silently from above or skitters across ceilings at unsettling speed
- Found in caves, ruins, old cellars, deep forest canopy, and anywhere dark and long undisturbed

**Tactics:**
- Positions on the ceiling before combat; drops with Ambush on the most isolated target
- Immediately follows with Web on whoever reacts or resists — wants targets immobile before biting
- Bites restrained targets repeatedly; retreats to ceiling when badly hurt
- Its lair floor may already be partially webbed — arriving players could begin restrained
