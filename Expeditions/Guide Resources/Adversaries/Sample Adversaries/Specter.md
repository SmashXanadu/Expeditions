## [Specter](#specter)

**Type:** Undead | **Size:** Medium

| HP | DPR | Atk | Mag | Def | Res |
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| 3  | 1   | 9   | 11  | 12  | 12  |

| Abilities | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| **Life Drain** | A cold spectral touch — deals magical damage. Armor provides no benefit against this attack. |
| **Terrify** | TN11 or flee from the Specter for one full round. |
| **Phase** | The Specter passes through one wall, floor, or obstacle as a free action on its turn. |

**Description:**
- A translucent shape holding the outline of a person, face locked in a silent scream, trailing cold black smoke
- Drifts without touching the floor, moving with the quiet wrongness of something that doesn't follow physics
- Found haunting locations tied to violent death — old battlefields, execution grounds, ruined houses

**Tactics:**
- Phases through a wall to appear behind the most isolated party member
- Opens with Terrify, then immediately follows with Life Drain on the fleeing target
- Ignores physical barriers entirely — walls and doors mean nothing
- Retreats by phasing through a floor or ceiling if badly pressed

| Consequences | Minor | Major |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **Attacking** | Physical weapons pass through — take 1 damage from the recoil of striking nothing<br>Drop your weapon<br>Disadvantage on next attack | Life force partially drained. Permanent disadvantage on attack rolls near Undead. |
| **Defending** | Its touch passes your armor — take 1 damage<br>Lose warmth in a piece of gear (it becomes unreliable in cold)<br>Disadvantage on next action | Soul-touched. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Sneaking** | It senses the living. Drop an item from paralyzed fingers<br>Take 1 damage<br>Disadvantage on next stealth roll | Marked in the spirit world. Permanent stealth disadvantage near Specters. |
| **Fleeing** | Drop an item<br>Take 1 damage — it follows through walls<br>Disadvantage on next action | Chased into a worse situation. Permanently lose 1 HP from max. |
| **Interacting** | Attempting to speak triggers Terrify — take 1 damage<br>Lose an action<br>Disadvantage on next social roll | Haunted. The Specter lingers near you until formally exorcised. |

| Loot (1d20) | Result |
|:--:|:--|
| 1–8 | Nothing physical — but the haunted area is now safe, which has its own value |
| 9–14 | A ghostlight fragment (faintly glowing shard that wards minor undead) |
| 15–18 | An echo-coin (replays the last conversation spoken near it when held) |
| 19–20 | The Specter's anchor object — what bound it to this place / a vision of its death containing a clue |
