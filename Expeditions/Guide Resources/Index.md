# Minor Consequences (d20)
Roll 1d20 when a **Minor Complication** is required.

1. Take 1 damage to your Health.
2. Drop a held item; it skitters into the next zone.
3. Your gear snags—lose 1 Durability on a random item.
4. You slip or stumble, making loud noise that draws attention.
5. Lose your footing; your next check is at Disadvantage.
6. A tool or pouch tears open—lose a random common item.
7. You twist something—your Speed is reduced by 1 for one zone.
8. A minor burn, scrape, or bruise; take 1 damage that cannot be redirected to gear.
9. You misjudge a step; fall prone or off-balance briefly.
10. You attract local creatures or environmental hazards.
11. Light blinks or flickers—vision becomes unreliable.
12. Your pack shifts—next Move becomes clumsy but still usable.
13. Something small breaks (strap, buckle, charm).
14. You kick up dust, ash, or spores—visibility drops temporarily.
15. A minor magical backlash if using magic—your next magic roll is -1.
16. A brief psychic echo disorients you; lose your next free interaction.
17. Something rattles loudly—enemies in adjacent zones become alert.
18. Pain flare; ignore your highest skill bonus for your next roll.
19. You sweat, panic, or hesitate—your next roll cannot benefit from Advantage.
20. Environmental flare (wind, heat, tremor) knocks you off balance; choose to take 1 damage or lose an item.

# Major Consequences (d20)
Roll 1d20 when a **Major Complication** is required.

1. Suffer a **grievous wound**; reduce maximum Health by 1 until Downtime recovery.
2. Lose a limb (arm, hand, leg, or eye); permanently affects combat or movement.
3. A piece of gear is destroyed instantly—choose one equipped item.
4. A spell, ability, or technique becomes unusable until completing a future quest or sacrifice.
5. Take 3 damage that cannot be absorbed by gear.
6. A magical surge corrupts the Core-holder or caster; gain a lingering curse.
7. Your Speed is reduced by 2 for the remainder of the Expedition.
8. A bone breaks—your next three physical checks are at Disadvantage.
9. You are knocked unconscious for one full round (or a scene if not in combat).
10. Lose a precious or unique item the moment matters most.
11. A Rift scar sears into you—roll Magic or suffer 2 permanent Resistance loss.
12. You become separated from the group (one or more zones away).
13. Your mind fractures under strain—permanently lose 1 point from a Skill of your choice.
14. A mutation manifests; gain a strange benefit and a strange drawback (Guide chooses).
15. Severe frostbite, burn, or poisoning; take ongoing 1 damage after every zone until treated.
16. You permanently lose access to a known ritual, spell, or magical effect.
17. Your gear fails catastrophically—armor and weapon both lose 2 Durability.
18. You incur a massive debt, obligation, or mark from a planar entity.
19. The injury brands you with a fate-tether; enemies sense you more easily for the rest of the Expedition.
20. Catastrophic misstep—fall into a hazard or collapsing terrain; take 4 damage and lose a random major item.

# Major Benefits (d20)
Roll 1d20 when a **Natural 20** grants a Major Benefit.

1. Gain 1 Health back (cannot exceed maximum).
2. Regain 1 Durability on a piece of gear of your choice.
3. Instantly reposition: move to any adjacent zone for free.
4. Spot a hidden detail, shortcut, or weakness—your next roll has Advantage.
5. Inspire an ally—choose one player to gain Advantage on their next roll.
6. Instantly stabilize difficult footing or terrain; ignore the next hazard you would trigger.
7. Recover a lost or dropped item from earlier in the Expedition.
8. Discover a useful clue or piece of information about the next zone.
9. Your strike or action is exceptionally effective; deal +2 bonus damage.
10. Disarm or disable something dangerous without needing a separate roll.
11. Momentum surge—your next Move this round is free.
12. Gain a burst of speed; increase Speed by 1 for the next two rounds/zones.
13. A piece of gear reacts or harmonizes with the moment; gain a temporary +1 bonus to a Skill for the next roll.
14. You avoid all complications, even optional ones you were about to accept.
15. You create an opening; the next enemy attack against you is at Disadvantage.
16. You counter or reflect a magical or environmental effect harmlessly.
17. You secure the perfect position or timing; your next ability costs no Charge.
18. Unexpected fortune—find a small consumable item (Guide chooses).
19. Overwhelming execution—your next attack or action deals maximum possible damage before modifiers.
20. Heroic surge—take an immediate extra Action (cannot be used to Move).
