## The Haunted Inn

**Difficulty:** 2 Star     |     **Duration:** 1 Hour

> "To any capable soul willing to earn an honest wage:
>
> My inn has not had a paying guest in three weeks. Candles snuff themselves. Food vanishes from locked pantries. My last two guests fled in the night claiming the walls were *breathing*.
>
> I am not a superstitious woman. Something is living in my walls and I want it out before the trade caravan arrives tonight.
>
> Discretion appreciated. I can't afford the reputation."
>
> — *Marta Holt, The Creaking Crow Inn*

\newpage

## The Haunted Inn
### Marta Holt

- **Description:** A stout, flour-dusted woman in her fifties with iron-grey hair pinned back and permanent worry lines around her eyes. Her apron has more patches than original cloth.
- **Goal:** Get the inn operational before tonight's caravan — her contract with the Bakers' Guild depends on it.
- **Voice:** Flat, practical, zero patience for dramatics. Cuts people off mid-sentence when she already knows the point.

**The Journey:** Down the main road, half a mile outside town...

- Roadside with three abandoned campsites — travelers who started toward the inn and thought better of it.
- A merchant's cart parked sideways in a ditch, horse tethered to a post, driver nowhere to be seen.
- The Creaking Crow visible ahead — two-story timber building, sign swinging on one hinge, every shutter drawn.

### The Location: The Creaking Crow Inn

- **Outside:** Salt-bleached timber framing, front door left ajar. The inn's sign is scorched at one corner despite no fire damage elsewhere.
- **Inside:** Common room with scattered tankards and a cold hearth. Kitchen shows ransacked pantry shelves — food stolen methodically, not smashed. Narrow stairs to four guest rooms above.
- **Atmosphere:** Musty grain and sour ale. A faint scratching moves through the walls at irregular intervals, stopping whenever anyone goes still to listen.

### The Situation: The Crawlspace Goblins

4 **Goblin Scouts** (Small) have colonized the crawlspace between the walls and floor. They moved in three weeks ago — not to cause trouble, but to hide from something in the east forest.

- **Health:** 1
- **Damage:** 1 / round
- **Attack** through small knotholes and gaps with improvised weapons from inside the walls.
- **Retreat** immediately into the crawlspace if struck — they know every tunnel.
- **Scatter** if more than two are defeated; attempt to flee through a hole in the kitchen floor.

**Possible Discoveries**

• **The Pantry Entrance** — A shelf unit swings outward. Scratch marks on the floor reveal it has been moved repeatedly. The crawlspace entrance is behind it.
• **The Nest** — A hoarded pile of stolen food, coins, and trinkets in the central crawlspace junction. Roughly 4 gold worth of mixed valuables.
• **Signs of Fear** — The goblins' eastern-facing wall is reinforced with scavenged nails and boards. Something scared them out of the forest. Tracks outside confirm they came from the east.
• **A Gnawed Letter** — A partially eaten merchant letter discusses a shipment rerouted through the inn — someone outside has been tracking Marta's cargo schedule.

**The Clock** : 1 Hour
A 12-wagon trade caravan is one hour out. If the inn isn't cleared, the caravan arrives to chaos — Marta loses her Guild contract and blames the party publicly.

**Escalations**

• **Knocked Lantern** — A goblin fleeing through the kitchen wall clips a lantern. Small fire starts on the pantry shelf.
• **Common Room Flood** — Panicked goblins burst through the floor into the common room all at once.
• **Marta Intervenes** — If the party is taking too long, Marta grabs a fireplace poker and enters the crawlspace herself.

**Rewards**: Week of free lodging at The Creaking Crow • 8 Gold • Marta's contact list (merchants, guild reps — a useful future resource)
