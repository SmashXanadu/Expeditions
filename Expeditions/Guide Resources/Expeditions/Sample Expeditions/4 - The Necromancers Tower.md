## The Necromancer's Tower

**Difficulty:** 4 Star     |     **Duration:** 1 Hour

> "I am writing this in haste and I beg you not to dismiss it as the ramblings of a frightened old priest.
>
> The graves of Millhaven are empty. Not disturbed — **empty**. Whatever walked out of them is still out there, and the tower on the hill is glowing green at night.
>
> We believe a young man named Corvin Ash took up residence in that tower a month ago. He seemed harmless. He was not harmless.
>
> I cannot pay much. But if this spreads to the town, no amount of gold will matter.
>
> Come before nightfall. Please."
>
> — *Father Aldric, Church of Millhaven*

## The Necromancer's Tower
### Father Aldric

- **Description:** A small, trembling man in his seventies with white stubble and mud on his boots from checking the graves again this morning. Clutches a holy symbol he clearly hasn't let go of in days.
- **Goal:** Stop the spreading undead influence before it reaches the town — and quietly, if possible. He doesn't want a panic.
- **Voice:** Soft and measured even when terrified. Chooses words carefully. Will not say "necromancer" out loud — calls it "the situation at the tower."

**The Journey:** North road out of Millhaven, up the hill...

- The churchyard at the town's edge: seventeen open graves. Clean-edged holes, no scattered dirt — they walked out deliberately.
- A skeleton patrol route is visible in the mud — circular tracks around the tower's base, worn deep.
- The tower visible against the sky, three stories of old stone. A single window on the top floor glows warm orange. The basement grate pulses green.

### The Location: The Tower at Millhaven Hill

- **Outside:** A squat stone tower surrounded by a low iron fence, gate hanging open. The surrounding grass is dead in a perfect circle. Basement-level iron grate breathes cold air upward.
- **Inside:** Ground floor study — shelves overturned, ritual chalk circles scratched into the floor, candles burning in green and blue. Stairs lead up to two residential floors and down to a stone basement.
- **Atmosphere:** Cold despite the season. Candles in wrong colors. A sound from the basement like slow, deliberate footsteps that never seem to get closer.

### The Situation: What Corvin Accidentally Made

**Ground Floor — 3 Skeleton Warriors** patrol inside and outside the tower. They are not under Corvin's control.

- **Health:** 3
- **Damage:** 1 / round
- **Advance** without hesitation toward any living creature they detect.
- **Reform** the first time each is reduced to 0 HP — collapses and rises again with 1 HP (fire or a called shot to the skull prevents this).
- **Ignore** social attempts entirely.

**Basement — 1 Specter** (the summoned entity, growing stronger)

- **Health:** 4
- **Damage:** 2 / round
- **Attack:** 11 | **Magic:** 13 | **Defense:** 14 | **Resistance:** 14
- **Life Drain** ignores armor — deals magical damage directly.
- **Terrify** TN13 or flee for one round.
- **Phase** through walls and floors freely.
- **Grows stronger** — every 20 minutes that passes, it gains +1 to all stats until destroyed or banished.

**Corvin Ash** is locked in the top floor. He is 19 years old, has not slept in four days, and will cooperate fully with anyone who knocks politely and does not immediately try to kill him.

**Possible Discoveries**

• **The Ritual Circle** — The summoning chalk in the basement is still partially intact. Disrupting it (TN12 or Corvin's help) reduces the Specter to its base stats permanently.
• **The Binding Anchor** — A cracked obsidian gem on the basement pedestal. Destroying it banishes the Specter instantly — but shattering it releases a shockwave (TN12 or take 2 damage).
• **Corvin's Journal** — His research notes reveal he was studying *protective* rituals, not destructive ones. The ritual book he found belonged to someone else — a name is mentioned that means nothing now but might later.
• **The Hidden Cache** — Behind a loose stone in the second floor: a small lockbox containing 12 gold, a vial of holy water, and a letter Corvin received that sent him down this path.

**The Clock** : 1 Hour
The Specter gains +1 to all stats every 20 minutes. At full strength it phases through the tower walls and walks toward Millhaven. Father Aldric's prayers will not stop it.

**Escalations**

• **Reinforcement** — The Specter's growing power animates one additional skeleton from the churchyard below. It arrives at the tower door.
• **Frost Floor** — The basement stones ice over. All movement in the basement requires a TN10 check or costs double.
• **Breach** — The Specter partially phases upward through the floor into the ground level study, splitting the party's attention.

**Rewards**: 30 Gold (church collection) • Corvin's assistance as a future contact (young mage, deeply in debt to the party) • The ritual book (dangerous, clearly valuable, clearly someone else's)
