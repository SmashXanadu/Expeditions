## [Battlefield Features](#battlefield-features)

Roll 1d20 or choose to add an interesting feature to a combat space.

| Roll | Feature |
|:-:|:--|
| 1–2 | **Elevation** — A ledge, balcony, or raised platform. Ranged attacks from above gain advantage. |
| 3–4 | **Pit or Drop** — An open hole or ledge edge. Knockdown effects carry extra risk. |
| 5–6 | **Pillars or Columns** — Solid cover. Blocks movement lanes and line of sight. |
| 7–8 | **Rubble or Debris** — Difficult terrain across a zone. Movement costs double. |
| 9–10 | **Unstable Floor** — Heavy impacts or knockdowns trigger TN12 or fall through. |
| 11–12 | **Fire Hazard** — Open flame, brazier, or burning section. Entering the zone costs 1 damage. |
| 13–14 | **Water** — Flooded zone. Movement halved; knockdown becomes a drowning risk. |
| 15–16 | **Interactive Object** — Lever, barrel, chandelier, or cage. Usable mid-combat with an action. |
| 17–18 | **Darkness** — Low or no light. Attacks and stealth both suffer disadvantage. |
| 19–20 | **Crowd or Chaos** — Civilians, prisoners, or panicked animals complicate everything. |
