# [Abilities](#abilities)
Abilities are unlocked by progressing through the **Hero Grid**. All abilities start with **3 Charges**.

## [Starter Abilities](#starter-abilities)
Starter abilities can be taken as your first node on the Hero Grid.

- **[Air]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Air/)** — Manipulate wind to move, push enemies, and deflect incoming attacks.
- **[Dark]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Dark/)** — Control shadows to teleport, phase through objects, and blind enemies.
- **[Water]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Water/)** — Shape water and ice to bind movement, redirect force, and control terrain.
- **[Fire]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Fire/)** — Conjure and direct flame to burn, reshape, and illuminate your surroundings.
- **[Earth]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Earth/)** — Reshape stone and soil to create cover, trigger tremors, and crush obstacles.
- **[Nature]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Nature/)** — Commune with the natural world to sense life, grow plants, and survive anywhere.
- **[Light]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Light/)** — Project pure light to reveal, cleanse, signal, and blind in equal measure.
- **[Armored]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Armored/)** — Use your armor's mass to hold ground, absorb blows, and dominate the frontline.
- **[Unarmored]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Unarmored/)** — Move with fluid precision to evade danger and strike from unexpected angles.
- **[Weapon Specialist]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/WeaponSpecialist/)** — Master your weapon's balance and potential to ensure every blow finds its mark.
- **[Spirit]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Spirit/)** — Attune to the unseen to sense emotions, project thoughts, and perceive the hidden.
- **[Electric]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Electric/)** — Channel electricity to shock, magnetize, and discharge devastating arcs of energy.

## [Advanced Abilities](#advanced-abilities)
Advanced abilities require an unlocked connected node to access.

- **[Shield]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Shield/)** — Block, soak, and redirect attacks to protect yourself and create safety for your allies.
- **[Protector]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Protector/)** — Force enemies to deal with you before they can reach anyone else.
- **[Disciplined]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Disciplined/)** — Anticipate every strike with calm precision, making yourself nearly impossible to pin down.
- **[Wild]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Wild/)** — Move with unpredictable ferocity and overwhelm enemies through relentless aggression.
- **[Brawler]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Brawler/)** — Use your body, the environment, and dirty tactics to keep opponents off-balance.
- **[Impact]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Impact/)** — Put your full weight behind every swing to shatter defenses and send enemies flying.
- **[Precision]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Precision/)** — Find and exploit the weakest point in any defense with surgical accuracy.
- **[Moon]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Moon/)** — Invoke lunar power to heal spirits, shroud the land, or unleash primal ferocity.
- **[Blood]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Blood/)** — Sense and control the flow of life, draining enemies and restoring allies.
- **[Star]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Star/)** — Call down cosmic fragments and astral energy to obliterate targets and empower allies.
- **[Volcanic]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Volcanic/)** — Crack the earth to release magma, ash, and scorching heat that reshapes terrain.
- **[Decay]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Decay/)** — Spread blight and toxic fumes that dissolve obstacles and leave enemies brittle and weak.
- **[Sun]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Sun/)** — Focus solar fire to sear away corruption, blind enemies, and empower your strikes.
- **[Undead]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Undead/)** — Command the fallen and tap into deathly resilience to persist long past your limits.
- **[Radiance]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Radiance/)** — Flood the area with holy brilliance that blinds enemies and emboldens allies.
- **[Renewal]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Renewal/)** — Mend wounds, purge poison, and restore the will to fight in yourself and your allies.
- **[Growth]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Growth/)** — Instantly grow vines, briars, and thickets to entangle enemies and reshape the battlefield.
- **[Animals]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Animals/)** — Borrow animal senses, traits, and allies to hunt, scout, and overpower your prey.
- **[Psychic]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Psychic/)** — Project thoughts, plant suggestions, and strike minds directly with psychic force.
- **[Illusion]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Illusion/)** — Weave convincing illusions of sight and sound to deceive, distract, and misdirect.
- **[Ice]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Ice/)** — Freeze terrain, enemies, and the air itself to control movement and shatter defenses.
- **[Storm]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Storm/)** — Unleash thunder, lightning, and gale-force winds to toss enemies and dominate the field.
- **[Explosion]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Explosion/)** — Detonate concentrated energy in devastating blasts that level structures and clear the field.
- **[Gravity]({{site.baseurl}}/PlayerResources/Abilities/Gravity/)** — Command gravitational force to pin enemies, leap great distances, and pull objects to hand.
