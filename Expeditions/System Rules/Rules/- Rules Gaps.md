# Rules Gaps

- [ ] **Advantage & Disadvantage** — Listed in the Rules Summary table with no explanation anywhere in the rules. When do you gain advantage? When do you gain disadvantage? What triggers each?
- [ ] **Complication & Benefit examples** — Rule 3 explains *when* complications trigger but never defines what a Minor vs Major complication actually looks like. A table or examples would help Guides apply them consistently.
- [ ] **Unconscious** *(Index)* — Rule 5 says dropping to 0 Health makes you Unconscious, but there are no rules for what that means. Can you be revived? By whom? Do you die after a number of rounds? Is there a death save or similar?
- [ ] **Broken Equipment** *(Index)* — Rule 5 and Rule 9 mention the Broken state but its only rule is "cannot be used." Are there other penalties?
- [ ] **Equip Gear action** — Rule 5 lists "Equip Gear" as a valid action on your turn but never explains what it covers. Does swapping weapons count? Pulling something from your Inventory? Putting on armor mid-combat?
- [ ] **Status effects** — Adversary abilities reference conditions like poison, fire, and fear, but no status effect rules exist anywhere in the rulebook. What do these conditions do mechanically?
- [ ] **Repair activity mechanics** — Downtime lists Repair as an optional activity but gives no rules for it. What skill do you use? Is there a TN? What materials are needed? Can you partially repair something?
- [ ] **Trade Skill usage mechanics** — Rule 12 lists Trade Skills but never explains how they work in practice. What's the resolution process? How do you use Blacksmith or Alchemist — roll against a TN? What materials are needed? What do you get?
- [ ] **Group / cooperative skill checks** — No rules exist for when multiple players attempt to help each other on a roll. Can a player assist? Does that grant advantage? Is there a group check option?
- [ ] **Expedition clock mechanics** — Rule 1 and Rule 4 mention 30- or 60-minute real-time expeditions with a "ticking clock," but there are no formal rules for what happens when time runs out, how escalations work, or how the Guide tracks pressure.
- [ ] **Shopping and item pricing** — Rule 10 mentions Gold as currency but there are no rules for where or how to spend it. Equipment lists presumably have prices, but no buying/selling rules exist in the rulebook.
