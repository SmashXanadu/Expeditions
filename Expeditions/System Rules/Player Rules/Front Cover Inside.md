# [About Expeditions](#about-expeditions)
## What is Expeditions?
Expeditions is a fast-paced tabletop roleplaying game that uses a D20 die to cooperatively create fun stories with a group of players. The goal of expeditions is to provide a timed, immersive experience that can be played by players of different experience levels at the same time without limiting the story or game play.

## What do I need to Play?

- 2+ players
- This "Player Rules" booklet
- A "Guide Rules" booklet
- Character sheets for each player
- Pencils and pens
- A 20-Sided Die

## What makes it different than other RPGs?

**Real-Time Expeditions**
	Sessions run for a set amount of real time — usually 30 or 60 minutes — with downtime phases in between. Expeditions fits your schedule the way a board game does: you know when it starts and when it ends.

**Streamlined Combat Rules**
	Combat moves efficiently with simple math and intuitive movement. Players are either close (melee), near (ranged), or far (cannot hit). No need to measure distance or count squares.

**Minimal Guide (GM) Prep**
	Rules and handouts are designed to minimize the Guide's prep time. Comprehensive tables and customizable scenarios let Guides run sessions on the fly while players handle their own rolls.

**Classless Specialization Grid**
	Players build characters without being restricted by class or race. Backgrounds, Loadouts, and Abilities can easily be combined to shape your character to match your vision.

**Player Driven Fate**
	Every roll is done by players and success is clearly defined. Players can push beyond their limits when the stakes demand it. Fate is in their hands. The Guide is the facilitator of their story, not the antagonist.
