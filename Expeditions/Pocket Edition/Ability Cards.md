## Air
*Connects to: Storm - Sun - Star - Moon*

**Manipulate the wind** to dash across the battlefield, hurl enemies backward, or deflect incoming attacks. **Channel violent gusts** into focused blasts, sustained updrafts, or swirling barriers that redirect movement and projectiles.

**Charges:** 3
### Example Actions:
- **Basic Action:** Stir the air to **carry a whisper across a room**, **slam shutters open**, **scatter loose papers**, or **extinguish fragile flames**.
- **Consume a Charge to**: 
	- Use a sharp updraft to double your jump height or safely break a long fall.
	- Blast a concentrated gust to shove a foe backward or knock them prone.
	- Create a swirling pocket of high pressure to veer incoming projectiles off-course.
	- Rapidly thin the air behind an enemy, dragging them toward you or into a hazard.
	- Suspend a falling heavy object, such as a collapsing stone pillar, in mid-air for several seconds.

## Explosion
_Requires Fire or Electric_

**Condense energy into a volatile point** before letting it detonate with a force that levels structures and clears the battlefield. **Trigger chain reactions** that turn a single spark into a series of rolling blasts, ensuring nothing is left standing in the wake of your fire.

**Charges:** 3
### Example Actions:
- **Basic Action:** Compress a tiny spark of unstable energy in your palm.
- **Consume a Charge to**:
    - Detonate a fiery blast that knocks enemies backward.
    - Trigger a chain reaction of smaller explosions.
    - Blast apart a door, wall, or barricade.
    - Launch an explosive projectile that detonates on impact.
    - Ignite every flammable object in a wide area.

## Gravity
_Requires Air or Earth_

**Command gravity itself** to make yourself light enough to leap great heights or heavy enough to crush the floor beneath you. **Create localized wells of force** that pin enemies to the dirt or pull distant objects into your waiting hand.

**Charges:** 3
### Example Actions:
- **Basic Action:** Make a small object feel slightly heavier or lighter in your hand.
- **Consume a Charge to**:
    - Increase gravity around an enemy, pinning them to the ground.
    - Make yourself nearly weightless and leap incredible distances.
    - Pull a distant object rapidly into your hand.
    - Slam a creature to the ground with crushing force.
    - Create a gravity well that drags nearby creatures toward its center.
