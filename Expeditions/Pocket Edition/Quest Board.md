## Expedition Title

**Difficulty:** X Star     |     **Duration:** X Hour

> “Write a short in-character request or notice that would appear on a quest board.
> 
> Keep it 2–4 short paragraphs. Focus on the **problem**, the **tone**, and the **promise of reward**.
>
> This text should make players curious and clearly communicate the type of job they are accepting.
>
> Sign the message with the name or title of the quest giver.”
>
> — *Quest Giver Name or Title*

\newpage

## Expedition Title
### Quest Giver Name

- **Description:** Brief physical description. Include 1–2 memorable visual details.
- **Goal:** What the NPC wants and why the problem matters to them.
- **Voice:** How they speak or sound. This helps the GM roleplay them quickly.

**The Journey:**  A short phrase describing the route or region the party travels through...

- Describe the environment the party moves through on the way to the location.
- Include a small encounter, complication, or strange sight.
- Include another short encounter, distraction, or moment of atmosphere.

### The Location: Name of the Site

- **Outside:** Describe what the location looks like from the outside. Include one strong visual detail.
- **Inside:** Describe the layout or obstacles within the location. Mention terrain, cover, or notable objects.
- **Atmosphere:** Describe lighting, smells, sounds, or other sensory details that establish mood.

### The Situation: Name of the Threat

X **Enemy Type** (size or brief description) inhabit or threaten the location.

- **Health:** X
- **Damage:** X / round
- **Behavior:** Describe one instinct or tactic the enemies follow.
- **Behavior:** Describe another instinct or tactic the enemies follow.
- **Behavior:** Describe how they react when injured, threatened, or defending something important.

**Possible Discoveries**

• **Clue or Object** — Something players may find while exploring.  
• **Clue or Object** — Something strange, valuable, or suspicious in the area.  
• **Hidden Feature** — A secret, tunnel, hidden door, or alternate route.  
• **Danger Sign** — Evidence of an impending threat or structural weakness.

**The Clock** : X Time Limit  
Describe what happens if the party takes too long or fails to resolve the situation.

**Escalations**

• **Environmental Change** — Terrain shifts, collapses, or becomes dangerous.  
• **Enemy Action** — Reinforcements arrive or enemies emerge from hiding.  
• **Warning Sign** — A dramatic sign that the clock is running out.

**Rewards**: Meal or Favor • Gold Amount • Useful Equipment or Supplies

## Warehouse Vermin Infestation

**Difficulty:** 1 Star     |     **Duration:** 1 Hour

> “The skittering in my warehouse walls isn't just annoying anymore — it's expensive. 
>  
> My grain stores are being eaten by things **much larger than rats**.
>
> It's dark, filthy work, but clear them out before my inventory becomes droppings.
>
> Stop by after and I'll see you fed and supplied.”
>
> — *Barnaby "Barn-Cat" Bumblewick*

\newpage

## Warehouse Vermin Infestation
### Barnaby "Barn-Cat" Bumblewick

- **Description:** A wiry, soot-stained man with a nervous twitch in his left eye. Wears an oversized leather apron.
- **Goal:** Protect his reputation with the Bakers' Guild at any cost.
- **Voice:** Raspy, hurried whispering; constantly clearing his throat.

**The Journey:**  Downtown past the docks...

- Sea-mist alleyways and a rickety boardwalk overlooking black harbor water
- A stray dog barking at shifting shadows. 
- A drunk sailor mistaking the party for a long-lost crew.

### The Location: Bumblewick Warehouse

- **Outside:** A leaning, salt-bleached wooden warehouse. The sliding door is jammed half-open with a rusted crowbar.
- **Inside:** A maze of waist-high grain sacks. The center of the room is a **kill zone** of spilled oats and shredded husks.
- **Atmosphere:** Deep shadows from **flickering lanterns**. The air reeks of toasted oats, briny rot, and wet fur. Constant *scritch-scratch* beneath the floorboards.

### The Situation: Warehouse Vermin

3 **Dire Rodents** (dog-sized) skitter through tunnels under the floor.

- **Health:** 3
- **Damage:** 1 / round
- **Defend** the scrap pile aggressively.
- **Retreat** into tunnels when badly wounded.
- **Swarm** anyone near the gnawed support pillar.

**Possible Discoveries**

• **Metal Hoard** — A nest pile of nails, buttons, and scrap metal.  
• **Strange Shard** — A faintly humming **metal fragment** buried in the scrap.  
• **Tunnel Network** — **Trap door** to a maze of tunnels beneath the warehouse floor.  
• **Gnawed Pillar** — The main support beam is heavily chewed.

**The Clock** : 1 Hour
If the infestation isn't cleared in time, the rodents chew through the **main support pillar**, collapsing the loft.

**Escalations**

• **Sack Collapse** — Grain sacks burst open, creating difficult terrain.  
• **Tunnel Ambush** — A rodent bursts from a new hole in the floor.  
• **Loft Creaking** — The overhead beams groan as the support pillar weakens.

**Rewards**: Hot Meal • 5 Gold • Supply Crate
