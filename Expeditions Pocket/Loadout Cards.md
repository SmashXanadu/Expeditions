## The Heavy Kit (**Durability:** 5)

**You carry the gear of a front-line survivor.** This loadout is designed for those who expect to take hits and need to deal them back. It prioritizes heavy materials, brute force, and the physical maintenance of steel.

- **+2 Reinforced Greataxe:** A massive, notched blade with a steel-wrapped haft.
- **Stonefoot Signet:** A heavy iron ring that makes your skin feel like rock.
- **Bastion Whetstone:** Used to keep a razor edge on heavy metal blades.
- **Bitter Tonic:** A thick liquid that numbs the body to physical pain.
- **Rusted Chain:** Ten feet of iron links that are nearly unbreakable.
- **Windproof Torches:** Thick bundles that refuse to go out in a storm.

---
## The Silent Kit (**Durability:** 3)

**You carry nothing that rattles or shines.** This kit is specialized for moving through restricted spaces and overcoming mechanical obstacles. Every item is blackened or muffled to ensure you remain a ghost in the machinery.

- **+1 Twin Daggers:** A pair of balanced blades with muffled leather grips.
- **Raven Pin:** A feather cloak fastener that dampens the sound of footsteps.
- **Fine Silk Rope:** Dark cord with a collapsible brass grappling hook.
- **Glass Dust:** A pouch of fine shards used to detect breath or movement.
- **Corrosive Acid:** A small vial capable of eating through iron lock tumblers.
- **Hollow Reed:** Used for breathing underwater or through narrow wall cracks.

---

## The Occult Kit (**Durability:** 3)

**You carry the tools of a dedicated investigator.** This loadout focuses on the extraction of information and the handling of dangerous curiosities. It is designed for those who solve problems with preparation rather than force.

- **Runed Quarterstaff:** An iron-bound pole etched with glowing sigils.
- **Amber Monocle:** A lens that reveals hidden magical signatures.
- **Specimen Jars:** Silver tweezers and glass bottles for sensitive samples.
- **Lead-Lined Box:** A secure container used to dampen magical radiation.
- **Scholar's Satchel:** A waterproof bag containing ink, pens, and parchment.
- **Sealed Scroll:** An ancient, unread map protected by a wax casing.

---

## The Wilderness Kit (**Durability:** 4)

**You carry the means to survive the elements.** This gear is weather-worn and practical, prioritizing utility and self-sufficiency. It is the perfect loadout for those who travel far from the safety of city walls.

- **+2 Sinew Hunting Bow:** A recurve shortbow with a grip of braided cord.
- **North Star Amulet:** A magnetic charm that always pulls toward the pole.
- **Bearskin Bedroll:** A heavy, treated wrap that keeps out the damp cold.
- **Volcanic Flint:** A tinderbox containing rare, self-striking stones.
- **Repellent Salve:** A pungent grease that keeps aggressive insects away.
- **Bone Hook:** A collapsible fishing pole with a hand-carved needle.

---

## The Noble Kit (**Durability:** 3)

**You carry the symbols of status and grace.** This equipment is designed to impress, charm, or deceive. It treats every encounter like a stage performance, providing the finery needed to control the narrative of a room.

- **+1 Filigree Rapier:** A slender dueling sword with a gold-plated hilt.
- **Jasmine Locket:** A silver pendant that emits a calming, floral scent.
- **Official Wax Kit:** A set of stamps for three different "official" seals.
- **Signal Mirror:** A polished glass used for flashes or checking corners.
- **Ever-Clean Silk:** A fine cloth that never stays stained or dirty.
- **Marked Deck:** A set of playing cards where every back has a hidden tell.

---
